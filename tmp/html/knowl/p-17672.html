\begin{equation*} \vF(x, y, z) = \big( 1 + z^{1+z^{1+z}}\,,\, 1 + z^{1+z^{1+z}}\,,\,1\big) \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} x^2 + y^2 = 1 - z^4 \end{equation*}

which is above the \(xy\)-plane. What is the flux of \(\vF\) downward through \(S\text{?}\)
