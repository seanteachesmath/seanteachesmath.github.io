The \(z\)-coordinate of the centre of mass is the weighted average of the \(z\)-coordinate over the cone. Since a density has not been specified, we assume that it is a constant. We may take the density to be \(1\text{,}\) so the \(z\)-coordinate of the centre of mass is \(\dblInt_S z\,\dee{S}/ \dblInt_S \dee{S}\text{.}\)
