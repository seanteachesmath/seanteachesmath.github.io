Solution 12.6.1.5.1.

To parametrize \(C\text{,}\) we note the vector between the two points is \((2-1,4-2,5-3)=(1,2,2)\text{.}\) So, the line is \((1,2,3)+t(1,2,2)\) for \(0 \le t \le 1\text{.}\) That is, \(x(t)=1+t\text{,}\) \(y(t)=2+2t\text{,}\) and \(z(t)=3+2t\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \int_C(xy+z)\dee{s}&=\int_0^1 \left((1+t)(2+2t)+(3+2t)\right)\sqrt{1^1+2^2+2^2}\dee{t}\\ &= \int_0^1 3\big(5 + 6t + 2t^2\big)\ \dee{t}=26 \end{align*}
