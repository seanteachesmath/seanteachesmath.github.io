The integral evaluations can be greatly simplified by using that the centroid \((\bar x,\bar y)\) of any region \(R\) in the \(xy\)-plane is

\begin{equation*} \bar x =\frac{\dblInt_R x\,\dee{x}\,\dee{y}}{\text{Area}(R)}\qquad \bar y =\frac{\dblInt_R y\,\dee{x}\,\dee{y}}{\text{Area}(R)} \end{equation*}
