\begin{align*} \diff{}{x} \sin x &= \cos x\\ \diff{}{x} \cos x &= -\sin x\\ \diff{}{x} \{-\sin x\} &= -\cos x\\ \diff{}{x} \{-\cos x\} &= \sin x\\ \diff{}{x} \sin x &= \cos x\\ \end{align*}

The fourth derivative is \(\sin x\) is \(\sin x\text{,}\) and the fourth derivative of \(\cos x\) is \(\cos x\text{,}\) so (a) and (b) are true.
