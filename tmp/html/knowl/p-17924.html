Let

  • \(R\) be a finite region in the \(xy\)-plane,

  • the boundary, \(C\text{,}\) of \(R\) consist of a finite number of piecewise smooth, simple closed curves

    • that are oriented (i.e. arrows are put on \(C\)) consistently with \(R\) in the sense that if you walk along \(C\) in the direction of the arrows, then \(R\) is on your left
  • \(F_1(x,y)\) and \(F_2(x,y)\) have continuous first partial derivatives at every point of \(R\text{.}\)

Then

\begin{equation*} \oint_{C} \big[F_1(x,y)\,\dee{x} +F_2(x,y)\,\dee{y}\big] =\dblInt_{R}\left(\frac{\partial F_2}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial F_1}{\partial y}\right)\ \dee{x}\dee{y} \end{equation*}
