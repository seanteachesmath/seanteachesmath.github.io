Problem: Evaluate \(\displaystyle\int (2x+1)^2 \dee{x}\text{.}\)

Work: We use the substitution \(u=2x+1\text{.}\) Then \(\dee{u}=2\dee{x}\text{,}\) so \(\dee{x} = \frac{1}{2}\dee{u}\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \int (2x+1)^2 \dee{x}&=\int u^2\cdot \frac{1}{2}\dee{u}\\ &=\frac{1}{6}u^3+C\\ &=\frac{1}{6}\left(2x+1\right)^3+C \end{align*}
