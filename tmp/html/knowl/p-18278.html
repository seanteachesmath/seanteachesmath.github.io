Two of the sides of the triangle are \((0,2,0)-(2,0,0)=(-2,2,0)\) and \((0,0,2)-(0,2,0)=(0,-2,2)\) so the area of the triangle is

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{2}\big|(-2,2,0)\times (0,-2,2)\big| =\frac{1}{2} \det\left[\begin{matrix}\hi&\hj&\hk\\ -2& 2& 0 \\ 0 & -2 & 2 \end{matrix}\right] =\frac{1}{2}\big|(4,4,4)\big| =2\sqrt{3} \end{equation*}
