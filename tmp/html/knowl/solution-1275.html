Solution 8.2.2.24.1.

We split the sum into two parts.

\begin{align*} \amp\sum_{n=2}^\infty \bigg( \frac{2^{n+1}}{3^n} + \frac1{2n-1} - \frac1{2n+1} \bigg)\\ &\hskip1in= \color{blue}{ \sum_{n=2}^\infty \frac{2^{n+1}}{3^n}} \quad \textcolor{black}{+}\quad \color{red}{ \sum_{n=2}^\infty \bigg( \frac1{2n-1} - \frac1{2n+1} \bigg) } \end{align*}

The first part is a geometric series.

\begin{align*} \color{blue}{ \sum_{n=2}^\infty \frac{2^{n+1}}{3^n} } &= \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{2^{n+3}}{3^{n+2}} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{2^3}{3^2}\cdot\left( \frac{2}{3}\right)^n\\ \end{align*}

We use LemmaÂ 8.2.5 with \(r=\dfrac{2}{3}\) and \(a=\dfrac{8}{9}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} &=\frac{8}{9}\cdot\frac{1}{1-\frac23}=\color{blue}{\frac{8}{3}} \end{align*}

The second part is a telescoping series. Let's make a table to see how it cancels.

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c||cc||r} \mathbf{n}&\mathbf{\frac{1}{2n-1}}&\mathbf{-\frac{1}{2n+1}}&\mathbf{s_n}\\ \hline 2&\frac{1}{3}&\color{red}{-\frac{1}{5}} &\frac{1}{3}-\color{red}{\frac{1}{5}}\\ 3&\color{red}{\frac{1}{5}} &\color{orange}{-\frac{1}{7}}&\frac{1}{3}-\color{orange}{\frac{1}{7}}\\ 4&\color{orange}{\frac{1}{7}} &\color{green}{-\frac{1}{9}}&\frac{1}{3}-\color{green}{\frac{1}{9}}\\ 5&\color{green}{\frac{1}{9}} &\color{blue}{-\frac{1}{11}}&\frac{1}{3}-\color{blue}{\frac{1}{11}}\\ 6&\color{blue}{\frac{1}{11}} &\color{purple}{-\frac{1}{13}}&\frac{1}{3}-\color{purple}{\frac{1}{13}}\\ 7&\color{purple}{\frac{1}{13}} &\color{black}{-\frac{1}{15}}&\frac{1}{3}-\color{black}{\frac{1}{15}}\\ \vdots&&& \end{array} \end{equation*}

After adding terms \(n=2\) through \(n=N\text{,}\) the partial sum is

\begin{align*} s_N&=\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{2N+1}\\ \end{align*}

because all the terms except the first part of the \(n=2\) term, and the last part of the \(n=N\) term, cancel. Then:

\begin{align*} \color{red}{\sum_{n=2}^\infty \bigg(\frac1{2n-1} - \frac1{2n+1}\bigg)} &=\lim_{N \to \infty}s_N = \lim_{N \to \infty}\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{2N+1}\\ &=\color{red}{\frac{1}{3}} \end{align*}

All together,

\begin{align*} \amp\sum_{n=2}^\infty \bigg( \frac{2^{n+1}}{3^n} + \frac1{2n-1} - \frac1{2n+1} \bigg)\\ &\hskip1in= \color{blue}{ \sum_{n=2}^\infty \frac{2^{n+1}}{3^n} } \quad \textcolor{black}{+} \quad \color{red}{ \sum_{n=2}^\infty \bigg( \frac1{2n-1} - \frac1{2n+1} \bigg) }\\ &\hskip1in=\textcolor{blue}{\frac83}+\textcolor{red}{\frac13}=3 \end{align*}
