Let \(S\) be the ellipsoid \(x^2+2y^2+3z^2=16\) and \(\hn\) its outward unit normal.

  1. Find \(\dblInt_S \vF\cdot\hn\,\dee{S}\) if \(\vF(x,y,z)=\dfrac{(x,y,z)-(2,1,1)}{{\big[(x-2)^2+(y-1)^2+(z-1)^2\big]}^{3/2}}\text{.}\)
  2. Find \(\dblInt_S \vG\cdot\hn\,\dee{S}\) if \(\vG(x,y,z)=\dfrac{(x,y,z)-(3,2,2)}{{\big[(x-3)^2+(y-2)^2+(z-2)^2\big]}^{3/2}}\text{.}\)
