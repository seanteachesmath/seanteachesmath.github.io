(g) For the vector field \(\vF\) to be conservative, we need

\begin{alignat*}{2} & & \frac{\partial\vF_1}{\partial y} &= \frac{\partial \vF_2}{\partial x}\\ &\iff\qquad & \frac{\partial }{\partial y}(ax+by) &= \frac{\partial }{\partial x}(cx+dy)\\ &\iff\qquad & b&=c \end{alignat*}

When \(b=c\text{,}\) an allowed potential is \(\frac{a}{2}x^2 +bxy + \frac{d}{2}y^2\text{.}\) The specified set is

\begin{gather*} \Set{(a,b,c,d)}{a,b,c,d\text{ all real and }b=c} \end{gather*}
