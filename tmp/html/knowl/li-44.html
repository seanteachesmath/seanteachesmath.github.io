Even and odd integers
\begin{align*} E &= \{ n | n \mbox{ is an even integer} \}\\ &= \{n | n =2k \mbox{ for some $k \in \mathbb{Z}$ } \}\\ &= \{2n | n \in \mathbb{Z}\}, \end{align*}
and similarly
\begin{align*} O &= \{ n | n \mbox{ is an odd integer} \}\\ &= \{2n+1 | n \in \mathbb{Z}\}. \end{align*}
