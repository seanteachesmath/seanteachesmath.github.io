(d) Observe that \(\vG=\vF+3ye^z\,\hk\text{,}\) with \(\vF\) evaluated with \(a=\pi,\ b=3\text{.}\) Hence

\begin{equation*} \int _C\vG\cdot \dee{\vr}=\int _C\vF\cdot \dee{\vr} +\int _C3ye^z\,\hk\cdot \dee{\vr} =-8+\int _C3ye^z\,\hk\cdot \dee{\vr} \end{equation*}

To evaluate the remaining integral, parametrize the curve by \(\vr(t) =t\hi+t\hj+\ln(1+t)\hk\) with \(0\le t\le 1\text{.}\) Then \(\vr'(t)=\hi+\hj+\frac{1}{1+t}\hk\) and \(3ye^z\hk=3t(1+t)\hk\) so that \(3ye^z\,\hk\cdot \dee{\vr}=3t\,\dee{t}\text{.}\) Subbing in

\begin{equation*} \int _C\vG\cdot \dee{\vr} =-8+\int_0^13t\,\dee{t} =-8+\frac{3}{2}=-\frac{13}{2} \end{equation*}
