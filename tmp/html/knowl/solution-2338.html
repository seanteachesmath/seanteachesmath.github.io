Solution 15.3.1.8.1.

Let's use Green's theorem. The rectangle, which we shall denote \(\cR\text{,}\) is

\(\cR=\set{(x,y)}{1\le x\le 3,\ 0\le y\le 1}\)

So Green's theorem gives

\begin{align*} &\oint_\cC\big(3y^2+2xe^{y^2}\big)\,\dee{x} + \big(2yx^2 e^{y^2}\big)\,\dee{y}\\ &\hskip1in=\dblInt_\cR \Big[ \pdiff{}{x}\big(2yx^2 e^{y^2}\big) -\pdiff{}{y}\big(3y^2+2xe^{y^2}\big) \Big]\,\dee{x} \dee{y}\\ &\hskip1in=\dblInt_\cR \Big[ 4xy e^{y^2} -6y -4xye^{y^2} \Big]\,\dee{x} \dee{y}\\ &\hskip1in = -6 \int_1^3 \dee{x}\int_0^1 \dee{y}\ y = -6 \int_1^3 \dee{x}\ \frac{1}{2}\\ &\hskip1in=-6 \end{align*}
