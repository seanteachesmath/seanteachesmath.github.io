Let \(f(r,\theta)=r^m\cos m\theta\) be a function of \(r\) and \(\theta\text{,}\) where \(m\) is a positive integer.

  1. Find the second order partial derivatives \(f_{rr}\text{,}\) \(f_{r\theta}\text{,}\) \(f_{\theta\theta}\) and evaluate their respective values at \((r,\theta)=(1,0)\text{.}\)
  2. Determine the value of the real number \(\la\) so that \(f(r,\theta)\) satisfies the differential equation
    \begin{equation*} f_{rr}+\frac{\la}{r}f_r+\frac{1}{r^2}f_{\theta\theta}=0 \end{equation*}
