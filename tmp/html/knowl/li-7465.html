As a second remark, suppose that the vector field \(\vF\) obeys \(\vnabla\times\vF=\vZero\) everywhere. Then Stokes' theorem forces \(\oint_{C}\vF\cdot \dee{\vr}=0\) are around all closed curves \(C\text{,}\) which implies that \(\vF\) is conservative, by Theorem 13.4.7. So Stokes' theorem provides another proof of Theorem 13.4.8.
