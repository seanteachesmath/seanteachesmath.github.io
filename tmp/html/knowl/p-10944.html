For fixed \(x\text{,}\) we recognize this as a geometric series with \(r=\frac{10-x}{10}\text{.}\) So it converges precisely when \(|r| \lt 1\text{,}\) i.e.

\begin{align*} \left| \frac{10-x}{10} \right|& \lt 1\\ \left|10-x \right|& \lt 10\\ -10 \lt x-10& \lt 10\\ 0 \lt x& \lt 20 \end{align*}

So, its interval of convergence is \((0,20)\text{.}\)
