As our leftmost strip is at \(x=0\) and our rightmost strip is at \(x=1\text{,}\) the total volume is
\begin{align*} \pi \int_0^1 (e^x+1)^2\,\dee{x} &=\pi \int_0^1 (e^{2x}+2e^x+1)\,\dee{x}\\ \amp=\pi\bigg[\frac{e^{2x}}{2}+2e^x + x\bigg]_0^1\\ &=\pi\bigg[\Big(\frac{e^{2}}{2}+2e + 1\Big) -\Big(\frac{1}{2}+2 + 0\Big)\bigg]\\ &=\pi\Big(\frac{e^{2}}{2}+2e -\frac{3}{2}\Big) \end{align*}
