(A2)

for each \(i\ne j\text{,}\) there is a function \(M_{i,j}(t)\) such that \(\vF_{i,j}(t)=M_{i,j}(t)\big[\vr_i(t)-\vr_j(t)\big]\text{.}\) In words, the force due to the rod joining particles \(i\) and \(j\) acts parallel to the line joining particles \(i\) and \(j\text{.}\) For (A1) to be true, that is to have \(M_{i,j}(t)\big[\vr_i(t)-\vr_j(t)\big] =-M_{j,i}(t)\big[\vr_j(t)-\vr_i(t)\big]\text{,}\) we need \(M_{i,j}(t)=M_{j,i}(t)\text{.}\)
