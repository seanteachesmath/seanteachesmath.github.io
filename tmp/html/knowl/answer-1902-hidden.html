(b) i. The top part is the part of the snowman's head that is inside the sphere

\begin{equation*} x^2+y^2+(z-4)^2 = 4 \end{equation*}

and above the cone

\begin{equation*} z-4 = - \sqrt{\frac{x^2+y^2}{3}}. \end{equation*}

ii. The middle part is the part of the snowman's head and body that is bounded on the top by the cone \(z-4 = - \sqrt{\frac{x^2+y^2}{3}}\) and is bounded on the bottom by the cone \(z = \sqrt{3(x^2+y^2)} \text{.}\)

iii. The bottom part is the part of the snowman's body that is inside the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2 = 12\) and is below the cone \(z = \sqrt{3(x^2+y^2)} \text{.}\)
