3.3.3.1.a The slope of the secant line is \(\dfrac{h(24)-h(0)}{24-0} \quad \dfrac{\mathrm{m}}{\mathrm{hr}}\text{;}\) this is the change in height over the first day divided by the number of hours in the first day. So, it is the average rate of change of the height over the first day, measured in meters per hour.
