Since the perimeter is required to be \(P\text{,}\) the height, \(h\text{,}\) of the rectangle must obey

\begin{align*} P&=\pi r+2r+2h\\ h&=\dfrac{1}{2}(P-\pi r -2r) \end{align*}

So the area is

\begin{align*} A(r)&=\half\pi r^2 +2rh\\ &=\half\pi r^2 +r(P-\pi r-2r)\\ &=rP-\half(\pi+4)r^2\\ \end{align*}

Finding all critical points:

\begin{align*} 0=A'(r)&=P-(\pi+4)r\\ r&=\dfrac{P}{\pi+4} \end{align*}
