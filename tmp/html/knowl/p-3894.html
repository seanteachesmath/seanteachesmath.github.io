Since \(f''(x)=6\text{,}\) the quadratic approximation (using \(f'(10)=60\text{,}\) \(f''(10)=6\text{,}\) and \(\Delta x = 1\)) tells us

\begin{equation*} \Delta y \approx f'(10)\Delta x+\frac{1}{2}f''(1)\left(\Delta x\right)^2= 60\cdot 1 +\frac{1}{2}(6)(1)^2= 63 \end{equation*}
