Exercise 6. M317 2015A.

Consider the following surfaces

  • \(S_1\) is the hemisphere given by the equation \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 4\) with \(z\ge 0\text{.}\)
  • \(S_2\) is the cylinder given by the equation \(x^2 + y^2 = 1\text{.}\)
  • \(S_3\) is the cone given by the equation \(z^2 = x^2 + y^2\) with \(z\ge 0\text{.}\)

Consider the following parameterizations:

  1. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta, \phi) =\big(\sqrt{4}\cos\theta\sin\phi\,,\, \sqrt{4}\sin\theta\sin\phi\,,\, \sqrt{4}\cos\phi\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 0\le\phi\le\pi/6 \end{align*}
  2. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta, \phi) =\big(\sqrt{4}\cos\theta\sin\phi\,,\, \sqrt{4}\sin\theta\sin\phi\,,\, \sqrt{4}\cos\phi\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 0\le\phi\le\pi/4 \end{align*}
  3. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta, \phi) =\big(\sqrt{4}\cos\theta\sin\phi\,,\, \sqrt{4}\sin\theta\sin\phi\,,\, \sqrt{4}\cos\phi\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 0\le\phi\le\pi/3 \end{align*}
  4. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta,z) = \big(\sqrt{4-z^2}\cos\theta\,,\,\sqrt{4-z^2}\sin\theta\,,\,z\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 1\le z\le 2 \end{align*}
  5. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta,z) = \big(\sqrt{4-z^2}\cos\theta\,,\,\sqrt{4-z^2}\sin\theta\,,\,z\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad \sqrt{2}\le z\le 2 \end{align*}
  6. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta,z) = \big(\sqrt{4-z^2}\cos\theta\,,\,\sqrt{4-z^2}\sin\theta\,,\,z\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad \sqrt{3}\le z\le 2 \end{align*}
  7. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta,z) = \big(z\cos\theta\,,\,z\sin\theta\,,\,z\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 0\le z\le 1 \end{align*}
  8. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta,z) = \big(z\cos\theta\,,\,z\sin\theta\,,\,z\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 0\le z\le \sqrt{2} \end{align*}
  9. \begin{align*} &\vr(\theta,z) = \big(z\cos\theta\,,\,z\sin\theta\,,\,z\big)\\ & 0\le\theta\le2\pi,\quad 0\le z\le \sqrt{3} \end{align*}
  10. \begin{align*} &\vr(x,y) =\big(x\,,\,y\,,\,\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\big)\\ & x^2+y^2\le 1 \end{align*}
  11. \begin{align*} &\vr(x,y) =\big(x\,,\,y\,,\,\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\big)\\ & x^2+y^2\le \sqrt{2} \end{align*}
  12. \begin{align*} &\vr(x,y) =\big(x\,,\,y\,,\,\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\big)\\ & x^2+y^2\le 2 \end{align*}

For each of the following, choose from above all of the valid parameterization of each of the given surfaces. Note that there may be one or more valid parameterization for each surface, and not necessarily all of the above parameterizations will be used.

  1. The part of \(S_1\) contained inside \(S_2\text{:}\)
  2. The part of \(S_1\) contained inside \(S_3\text{:}\)
  3. The part of \(S_3\) contained inside \(S_2\text{:}\)
  4. The part of \(S_3\) contained inside \(S_1\text{:}\)
