As a check, we observe that the derivative of the answer

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x} \left( \frac{\sqrt{x^2-9}}{9x}+C\right) & =-\frac{\sqrt{x^2-9}}{9x^2} + \frac{x}{9x\sqrt{x^2-9}}\\ &=\frac{1}{9}\ \frac{-(x^2-9)+x^2}{x^2\sqrt{x^2-9}} =\frac{1}{x^2\sqrt{x^2-9}} \end{align*}

is exactly the integrand. (We remark that this is the case even for \(x\le -3\text{.}\))
