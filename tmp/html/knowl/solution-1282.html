Solution 8.2.2.31.1.

Let's make a table. Keep in mind \(\cos^2\theta+\sin^2\theta=1\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c||cc||c} \mathbf{n}&\mathbf{\dfrac{\sin^2n}{2^n}}&\mathbf{\dfrac{\cos^2(n+1)}{2^{n+1}}}&\mathbf{s_n}\\ \hline 3&\dfrac{\sin^23}{2^3}&\textcolor{red}{\dfrac{\cos^24}{2^4}} & \dfrac{\sin^23}{2^3}+\textcolor{red}{\dfrac{\cos^24}{2^4}} \\ 4&\textcolor{red}{\dfrac{\sin^24}{2^4}} &\textcolor{green}{\dfrac{\cos^25}{2^5}} & \dfrac{\sin^23}{2^3}+\textcolor{red}{\dfrac{1}{2^4}}+\textcolor{green}{\dfrac{\cos^25}{2^5}} \\ 5&\textcolor{green}{\dfrac{\sin^25}{2^5}} &\textcolor{blue}{\dfrac{\cos^26}{2^6}} & \dfrac{\sin^23}{2^3}+\textcolor{red}{\dfrac{1}{2^4}}+\textcolor{green}{\dfrac{1}{2^5}}+\textcolor{blue}{\dfrac{\cos^26}{2^6}} \\ 6&\textcolor{blue}{\dfrac{\sin^26}{2^6}} &\textcolor{orange}{\dfrac{\cos^27}{2^7}} & \dfrac{\sin^23}{2^3}+\textcolor{red}{\dfrac{1}{2^4}}+\textcolor{green}{\dfrac{1}{2^5}} +\textcolor{blue}{\dfrac{1}{2^6}}+\textcolor{orange}{\dfrac{\cos^27}{2^7}}\\ 7&\textcolor{orange}{\dfrac{\sin^27}{2^7}} &\textcolor{brown}{\dfrac{\cos^28}{2^8}} & \dfrac{\sin^23}{2^3}+ \textcolor{red}{\dfrac{1}{2^4}} + \textcolor{green}{\dfrac{1}{2^5}}+ \textcolor{blue}{\dfrac{1}{2^6}}+ \textcolor{orange}{\dfrac{1}{2^7}}+\textcolor{brown}{\dfrac{\cos^28}{2^8}}\\ \vdots&&& \end{array} \end{equation*}

This gives us an equation for the partial sum \(s_N\text{,}\) when \(N \geq 4\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} s_N&=\sum_{n=3}^N\left(\frac{\sin^2 n}{2^n}+\frac{\cos^2(n+1)}{2^{n+1}} \right)\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{2^3} +\left( \sum_{n=4}^{N}\frac{1}{2^n} \right)+\frac{\cos^2(N+1)}{2^{N+1}}\\ \end{align*}

Using Definition 8.2.3, our series evaluates to:

\begin{align*} \lim_{N \to \infty}s_N &=\lim_{N \to \infty}\left[\frac{\sin^23}{2^3} +\left( \sum_{n=4}^{N}\frac{1}{2^n} \right)+\frac{\cos^2(N+1)}{2^{N+1}}\right]\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\left[\lim_{N \to \infty}\frac{\cos^2(N+1)}{2^{N+1}}\right] +\sum_{n=4}^\infty \frac{1}{2^n}\\ \end{align*}

We evaluate the limit using the squeeze theorem; the series is geometric.

\begin{align*} &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+0+\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{2^{n+4}}\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\frac{1}{2^4}\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{2^{n}}\\ \end{align*}

Using Lemma 8.2.5,

\begin{align*} &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\frac{1}{2^4} \frac{1}{1-\frac12}\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\frac{1}{8} \approx 0.1275 \end{align*}
