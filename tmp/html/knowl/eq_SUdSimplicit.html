Equation 14.3.3.

For the surface \(G(x,y,z)=K\text{,}\) when \(G_z(x,y,z)\ne 0\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \hn\, \dee{S} & = \pm\frac{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)}{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)\cdot\hk}\ \dee{x}\dee{y}\\ \dee{S}&= \bigg|\frac{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)}{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)\cdot\hk}\bigg|\ \dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}

Similarly, for the surface \(G(x,y,z)=K\text{,}\) when \(G_x(x,y,z)\ne 0\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \hn\, \dee{S} & = \pm\frac{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)}{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)\cdot\hi}\ \dee{y}\dee{z}\\ \dee{S}&= \bigg|\frac{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)}{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)\cdot\hi}\bigg|\ \dee{y}\dee{z} \end{align*}

and for the surface \(G(x,y,z)=K\text{,}\) when \(G_y(x,y,z)\ne 0\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \hn\, \dee{S} & = \pm\frac{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)}{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)\cdot\hj}\ \dee{x}\dee{z}\\ \dee{S}&= \bigg|\frac{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)}{\vnabla G\big(x,y,z\big)\cdot\hj}\bigg|\ \dee{x}\dee{z} \end{align*}
