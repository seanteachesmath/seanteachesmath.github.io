Consider the vector field

\begin{equation*} \vF(x, y, z) = \frac{x-2y}{x^2+y^2}\,\hi +\frac{2x + y}{x^2+y^2}\,\hj + z\,\hk \end{equation*}
  1. Determine the domain of \(\vF\text{.}\)
  2. Compute \(\vnabla \times \vF\text{.}\) Simplify the result.
  3. Evaluate the line integral
    \begin{equation*} \int_C \vF \cdot \dee{\vr} \end{equation*}
    where \(C\) is the circle of radius \(2\) in the plane \(z = 3\text{,}\) centered at \((0, 0, 3)\) and traversed counter-clockwise if viewed from the positive \(z\)-axis, i.e. viewed “from above”.
  4. Is \(\vF\) conservative?
