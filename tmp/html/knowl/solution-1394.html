Solution 8.6.7.15.1.

Recall that

\begin{equation*} \arctan(y) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty(-1)^n\frac{y^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \end{equation*}

Setting \(y=2x\text{,}\) we have

\begin{align*} \int x^4\arctan(2x) \,\dee{x}&= \int\left( x^4\sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^n\frac{(2x)^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \right)\dee{x}\\ &= \int\left( \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^n\frac{2^{2n+1}x^{2n+5}}{2n+1} \right)\dee{x}\\ &= \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^n\frac{2^{2n+1} x^{2n+6}}{(2n+1)(2n+6)} +C\\ &=\sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^n\frac{2^{2n} x^{2n+6}}{(2n+1)(n+3)} +C \end{align*}
