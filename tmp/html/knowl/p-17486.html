(c) Recalling that \(\vnabla^2 = \vnabla\cdot\vnabla\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \vnabla^2 (r^k ) &=\vnabla\cdot\big(\vnabla(r^k)\big)\\ &=\vnabla\cdot(k r^{k-2}\,\vr) &\text{by part (a)}\\ &=k(3+k-2)r^{k-2} &\text{by part (b), but with $k$ replaced by $k-2$} \end{align*}

We want \(k=-2\text{.}\)
