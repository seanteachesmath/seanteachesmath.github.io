\begin{align*} f(x) &\approx f(1)+f'(1)(x-1)\\ \end{align*}

So, we calculate:

\begin{align*} f(1)&=\log(1)=0\\ f'(x)&=\frac{1}{x}\\ f'(1)&=\frac{1}{1}=1\\ \end{align*}

Therefore,

\begin{align*} f(x) &\approx 0+1(x-1)=x-1\\ \end{align*}

When \(x=0.93\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} f(0.93) &\approx 0.93-1=-0.07 \end{align*}
