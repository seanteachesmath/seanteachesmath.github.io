Let \(G\) be the region in \(\bbbr^2\) given by

\begin{gather*} x^2 + y^2 \le 1\\ 0 \le x \le 2y\\ y \le 2x \end{gather*}
  1. Sketch the region \(G\text{.}\)
  2. Express the integral \(\dblInt_G f(x,y)\ \dee{A}\) a sum of iterated integrals \(\dblInt f(x, y)\ \dee{x}\dee{y}\text{.}\)
  3. Express the integral \(\dblInt_G f(x,y)\ \dee{A}\) as an iterated integral in polar coordinates \((r, \theta)\) where \(x = r \cos(\theta)\) and \(y = r \sin(\theta)\text{.}\)
