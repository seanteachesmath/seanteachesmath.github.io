Item.

The third uses arc length from \((r,0)\) as the parameter. We have seen, in Example 9.6.14, that the arc length from \((r,0)\) to \(\vr_1(\theta)\) is just \(s=r\theta\text{.}\) So the point on the semicircle that is arc length \(s\) away from \((r,0)\) is

\begin{align*} &\vr_3(s) = \vr_1\Big(\frac{s}{r}\Big)\\ &= \Big(r\cos\frac{s}{r}\,,\,r\sin\frac{s}{r}\Big)\\ &0\le s\le \pi r \end{align*}
