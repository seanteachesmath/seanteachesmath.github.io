Let

\begin{equation*} \vF(x, y, z) = \Big(\frac{y}{x} +x^{1+x^2}\,,\, x^2-y^{1+y^2}\,,\, \cos^5(\ln z)\Big) \end{equation*}
  1. Write down the domain \(D\) of \(\vF\text{.}\)

  2. Circle the correct statement(s):

    1. D is connected.
    2. D is simply connected.
    3. D is disconnected.
  3. Compute \(\vnabla\times\vF\text{.}\)
  4. Let \(C\) be the square with corners \((3 \pm 1, 3 \pm 1)\) in the plane \(z = 2\text{,}\) oriented clockwise (viewed from above, i.e. down \(z\)-axis). Compute
    \begin{equation*} \int_C \vF \cdot \dee{\vr} \end{equation*}
  5. Is \(\vF\) conservative?
