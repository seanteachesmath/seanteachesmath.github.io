Let \(S\) be the solid formed by rotating the parabola \(y=x^2\) from \(x=0\) to \(x=2\) about the \(x\)-axis.

  1. What is the average area of the circular cross-sections of \(S\text{?}\) Call this value \(A\text{.}\)
  2. What is the volume of \(S\text{?}\)
  3. What is the volume of a cylinder with circular cross-sectional area \(A\) and length 2?
