(c) To get a part of \(S_3\text{,}\) we need to use one of the parametrizations G, H, I, J, K, L. In the cases of G, H, I, for \(\vr(\theta, z) =x(\theta, z)\,\hi+ y(\theta, z)\,\hj+z(\theta, z)\,\hk\) to lie inside \(S_2\) we need (recalling that all points of \(S_3\) have \(z\ge 0\))

\begin{align*} x(\theta, z)^2 + y(\theta, z)^2 \le 1 &\iff z^2 \le 1 \iff 0\le z\le 1 \end{align*}

In the cases of J, K, L, for \(\vr(x, y) =x(x, y)\,\hi+ y(x, y)\,\hj+z(x, y)\,\hk\) to lie inside \(S_3\) we need

\begin{align*} x(x, y)^2 + y(x, y)^2 \le 1 &\iff x^2+y^2 \le 1 \end{align*}

So parametrizations G and J work.
