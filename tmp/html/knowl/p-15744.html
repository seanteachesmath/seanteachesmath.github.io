(a) In particular, a (non unit) tangent vector at \(\vr(-1) = (1, -1, -1)\) is \(\vr'(-1) = (-2, 1,3)\text{.}\) So the tangent line to the curve at \((1, -1, -1)\) is

\begin{gather*} (x,y,z) - (1, -1, -1) = t (-2, 1, 3) \end{gather*}

or

\begin{align*} x&=1-2t\\ y&=-1+t\\ z&=-1+3t \end{align*}
