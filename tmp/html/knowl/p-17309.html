(b) The specified integral is

\begin{align*} &\dblInt_S \big(xy\,\hi + xz\,\hj + zy\,\hk\big)\cdot\hn\,\dee{S}\\ &\hskip0.25in= \dblInt_{x^2+z^2\le 1}\dee{x}\dee{z}\ \big(x\sqrt{2-x^2-z^2}\,,\, xz\,,\, z\sqrt{2-x^2-z^2}\big)\cdot\\ &\hskip2.5in \left(\frac{x}{\sqrt{2-x^2-z^2}}\,,1\,,\, \frac{z}{\sqrt{2-x^2-z^2}}\right)\\ &\hskip0.25in= \dblInt_{x^2+z^2\le 1}\dee{x}\dee{z}\ \big\{x^2 +xz +z^2 \big\} \end{align*}

Switching to polar coordinates with \(x=r\cos\theta\) and \(z=r\sin\theta\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} &\dblInt_S \big(xy\,\hi + xz\,\hj + zy\,\hk\big)\cdot\hn\,\dee{S}\\ &\hskip1in=\int_0^{2\pi}\dee{\theta}\int_0^1\dee{r}\ r(r^2\cos^2\theta +r^2\sin\theta\cos\theta +r^2\sin^2\theta)\\ &\hskip1in= \left[\int_0^1\dee{r}\ r^3\right] \left[\int_0^{2\pi}\dee{\theta}\ \big(1+\sin\theta\cos\theta\big)\right]\\ &\hskip1in= \left[\frac{r^4}{4}\right]_0^1 \left[\theta+\frac{\sin^2\theta}{2}\right]_0^{2\pi}\\ &\hskip1in= \frac{1}{4}\times (2\pi) =\frac{\pi}{2} \end{align*}
