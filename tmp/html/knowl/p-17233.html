Let \(S\) be the surface obtained by revolving the curve \(z = e^y\) , \(0 \le y \le 1\text{,}\) around the \(y\)-axis, with the orientation of \(S\) having \(\hn\) pointing toward the \(y\)-axis.

  1. Draw a picture of \(S\) and find a parameterization of \(S\text{.}\)
  2. Compute the integral \(\dblInt_S e^y\, \dee{S}\text{.}\)
  3. Compute the flux integral \(\dblInt_S\vF\cdot\hn\,\dee{S}\) where \(\vF= (x, 0, z)\text{.}\)
