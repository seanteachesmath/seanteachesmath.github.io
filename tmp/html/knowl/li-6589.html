Item i.
\(|\vv|\) hits 0, in which case you stop rising and start descending. The speed \(|\vv|\) is zero when \(y=y_S=\frac{E}{mg}\text{.}\) (The subscript “\(S\)” stands for “stop”.) Physicists say that when you reach \(y_S\) all of your kinetic energy (\(\frac{1}{2}m|\vv|^2\)) has been converted into potential energy (\(mgy\)).
