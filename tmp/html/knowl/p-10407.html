It is known that \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{12}\) (you don't have to show this). Find \(N\) so that \(S_N\text{,}\) the \(N^{\rm th}\) partial sum of the series, satisfies \(| \frac{\pi^2}{12} - S_N | \le 10^{-6}\text{.}\) Be sure to say why your method can be applied to this particular series.
