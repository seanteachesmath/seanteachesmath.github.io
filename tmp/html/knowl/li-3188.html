Near the surface of the Earth gravity exerts a downward force of \(mg\) on a body of mass \(m\text{.}\) The constant \(g=9.8\)m/\(\mathrm{sec}^2\) is called the standard acceleration due to gravity  9 . For us to raise the pancake we have to apply a compensating upward force of \(mg\text{,}\) which, for our pancake, is
\begin{align*} F &= g \rho \times \pi r^2\dee{x} \end{align*}
