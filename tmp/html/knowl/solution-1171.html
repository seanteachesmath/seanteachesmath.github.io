Solution 7.3.3.19.1.

Using EquationÂ 7.3.5, the \(x\)-coordinate of the centroid is

\begin{align*} \bar x&=\frac{\int_0^1 x\frac{1}{1+x^2}\,\dee{x}}{\int_0^1\frac{1}{1+x^2}\,\dee{x}}\\ \end{align*}

We can guess the antiderivative in the numerator, or use the substitution \(u=1+x^2\text{,}\) \(\dee{u}=2x\,\dee{x}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} &=\frac{\half\log(1+x^2)\big|_0^1}{\arctan x\big|_0^1} =\frac{\half\log 2}{\pi/4} =\frac{2}{\pi}\log 2 \approx 0.44127 \end{align*}
