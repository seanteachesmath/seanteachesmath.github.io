Solution 15.2.6.7.1.

(a) The divergence is

\begin{align*} \vnabla\cdot\vF &= \pdiff{}{x}\big(z+\sin y\big) +\pdiff{}{y}\left(zy\right) +\pdiff{}{z}\big(\sin x\cos y\big)\\ &= z \end{align*}

(b) Let

\begin{gather*} V=\Set{(x,y,z)}{x^2+y^2+z^2\le 9} \end{gather*}

By the divergence theorem (assuming that we are to find the outward flux),

\begin{align*} \dblInt_{\partial V} \vF\cdot\hn\,\dee{S} &= \tripInt_V \vnabla\cdot \vF\ \dee{V} = \tripInt_V z\ \dee{V} = 0 \end{align*}

since \(z\) is odd.
