\begin{equation*} \vF = (-y+e^x\sin x)\,\hi +y^4\,\hj +\sqrt{z}\tan z\,\hk \end{equation*}

Evaluate the integral \(\int_C\vF\cdot\dee{\vr}\text{.}\)
