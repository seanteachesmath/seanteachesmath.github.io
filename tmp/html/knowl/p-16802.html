The vector field \(\vF(x,y,z)= Ax^3y^2z\,\hi+\big(z^3+Bx^4yz\big)\,\hj +\big(3yz^2-x^4y^2\big)\,\hk\) is conservative on \(\bbbr^3\text{.}\)

  1. Find the values of the constants \(A\) and \(B\text{.}\)
  2. Find a potential \(\varphi\) such that \(\vF=\vnabla\varphi\) on \(\bbbr^3\text{.}\)
  3. If \(\cC\) is the curve \(y=-x,\ z=x^2\) from \((0,0,0)\) to \((1,-1,1)\text{,}\) evaluate \(I=\int_\cC \vF\cdot \dee{\vr}\text{.}\)
  4. Evaluate \(J=\int_\cC (z-4x^3y^2z)\dee{x}+(z^3-x^4yz)\dee{y}+(3yz^2-x^4y^2)\dee{z}\text{,}\) where \(\cC\) is the curve of part (c).
  5. Let \(\cT\) be the closed triangular path with vertices \((1,0,0)\text{,}\) \((0,1,0)\) and \((0,0,1)\text{,}\) oriented counterclockwise as seen from the point \((1,1,1)\text{.}\) Evaluate \(\int_\cT(z\hi+\vF)\cdot \dee{\vr}\text{.}\)
