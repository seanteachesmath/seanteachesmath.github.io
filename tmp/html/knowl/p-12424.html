Define, for all \((x,y)\ne(0,0)\text{,}\) \(f(x,y)=\frac{x^2y}{x^4+y^2}\text{.}\)

  1. Let \(0\le \theta \lt 2\pi\text{.}\) Compute \(\ds\lim_{r\rightarrow 0^+}f(r\cos\theta,r\sin\theta)\text{.}\)
  2. Compute \(\ds\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}f(x,x^2)\text{.}\)
  3. Does \(\ds\lim_{(x,y)\rightarrow (0,0)}f(x,y)\) exist?
