\begin{align*} \tan(X+\pi)&=\tan(X)&&\mbox{for any $X$ in the domain of $\tan X$}\\ \end{align*}

Replacing \(X\) with \(\pi x\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \tan(\pi x + \pi)&=\tan(\pi x)&&\mbox{for any $x$ in the domain of $\tan(\pi x)$}\\ \tan(\pi(x+1))&=\tan(\pi x)&&\mbox{for any $x$ in the domain of $\tan(\pi x)$}\\ f(x+1)&=f(x)&&\mbox{for any $x$ in the domain of $\tan(\pi x)$} \end{align*}
