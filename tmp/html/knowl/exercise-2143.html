Exercise 16.

Evaluate, for each of the following, the flux \(\ \dblInt_S\vF\cdot\hn \,\dee{S}\ \) where \(\hn\) is the outward normal to the surface \(S\text{.}\)

  1. \(\ \vF={(x^2+y^2+z^2)}^n(x\,\hi+y\,\hj+z\,\hk)\ \) and the surface \(S\) is the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2=a^2\text{.}\)
  2. \(\ \vF=x\,\hi+y\,\hj+z\,\hk\ \) and \(S\) is the surface of the rectangular box \(0\le x\le a\text{,}\) \(0\le y\le b\text{,}\) \(0\le z\le c\text{.}\)
  3. \(\ \vF=y\,\hi+z\,\hk\ \) and \(S\) is the surface of the solid cone \(0\le z\le 1-\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\text{.}\)
