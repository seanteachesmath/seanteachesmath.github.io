This does leave the problem of proving the arithmetic of limits and the limits of the basic building blocks. The proof of the Theorem 2.4.3 is quite involved and we leave it to the very end of this Chapter. Before we do that we will prove Theorem 2.4.1 by a formal \(\epsilon\)–\(\delta\) proof. Then in the next section we will look at the formal definition of limits at infinity and prove Theorem 2.5.3. The proof of the Theorem 2.5.9, the arithmetic of infinite limits, is very similar to that of Theorem 2.4.3 and so we do not give it.
