And just a reminder of some of its  8  properties…

  1. \(\displaystyle e^0=1\)
  2. \(\displaystyle e^{x+y}=e^xe^y\)
  3. \(\displaystyle e^{-x}=\tfrac{1}{e^x}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \big(e^x\big)^y=e^{xy}\)
  5. \(\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow\infty}e^x=\infty\text{,}\) \(\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow-\infty}e^x=0\)
