Exercise 11. M317 2011A.

Consider the motion of a thumbtack stuck in the tread of a tire which is on a bicycle moving at constant speed. This motion is given by the parametrized curve

\begin{equation*} \vr(t) = \big(t - \sin t\,,\, 1 - \cos t\big) \end{equation*}

with \(t \gt 0\text{.}\)

  1. Sketch the curve in the \(xy\)-plane for \(0 \lt t \lt 4\pi\text{.}\)
  2. Find and simplify the formula for the curvature \(\kappa(t)\text{.}\)
  3. Find the radius of curvature of the osculating circle to \(\vr(t)\) at \(t = \pi\text{.}\)
  4. Find the equation of the osculating circle to \(\vr(t)\) at \(t = \pi\text{.}\)
