\begin{align*} \lim_{h\to 0} \left[ F(h) \cdot g(x) - f(x)\cdot G(h)\right] &= \lim_{h\to0} F(h)\cdot g(x) - \lim_{h\to 0} f(x)\cdot G(h)\\ \end{align*}

Now because \(f(x),g(x)\) do not change with \(h\) we can factor them out.

\begin{align*} &= g(x) \lim_{h\to0} F(h) - f(x) \lim_{h\to 0} G(h)\\ &= g(x) f'(x) - f(x) g'(x) \end{align*}
