(c) As preliminary computations, let's find

\begin{align*} \pdiff{}{x}\left(\frac{-z}{x^2+z^2}\right) &=-\frac{2x(-z)}{{(x^2+z^2)}^2} =\frac{2xz}{{(x^2+z^2)}^2}\\ \pdiff{}{z}\left(\frac{x}{x^2+z^2}\right) &=-\frac{2z(x)}{{(x^2+z^2)}^2} =\frac{-2xz}{{(x^2+y^2)}^2} \end{align*}

So the divergence of \(\vF\) is

\begin{align*} \vnabla\cdot\vF &=\pdiff{}{x}\left(\frac{-z}{x^2+z^2}\right) +\pdiff{}{y}\left(y\right) + \pdiff{}{z}\left(\frac{x}{x^2+z^2}\right) =1 \end{align*}
