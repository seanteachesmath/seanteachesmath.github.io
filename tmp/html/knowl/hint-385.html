Hint 4.2.2.18.1.

The easiest way to figure out the area of the sector of an annulus (or a circle) is to figure out the area of the entire annulus, then multiply by what proportion of the entire annulus the sector is. For example, if your sector is \(\frac{1}{10}\) of the entire annulus, then its area is \(\frac{1}{10}\) of the area of the entire annulus.
