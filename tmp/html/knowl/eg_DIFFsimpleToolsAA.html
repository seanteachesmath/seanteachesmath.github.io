Example 3.6.1. \(\diff{}{x} \{ 4x + 7 \} \).
\begin{align*} \diff{}{x} \{ 4x + 7 \} &= 4 \cdot \diff{}{x} \{ x \} + 7 \cdot \diff{}{x} \{ 1 \} & \text{LIN}\\ &= 4 \cdot 1 + 7 \cdot 0 = 4 \end{align*}

where we have used LIN with \(f(x)=x\text{,}\) \(g(x)=1\text{,}\) \(\alpha=4\text{,}\) \(\beta=7\text{.}\)
