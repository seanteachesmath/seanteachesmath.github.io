\begin{align*} x^2-y\cos(uv)&=v\\ x^2+y^2-\sin(uv)&=\frac{4}{\pi}u \end{align*}

  1. Find
    \begin{equation*} \pdiff{x}{u}\text{ and } \pdiff{y}{u} \end{equation*}
    at \((u,v)=\big(\frac{\pi}{2},0\big)\text{.}\)
  2. If \(z=x^4+y^4\text{,}\) determine \(\pdiff{z}{u}\) at the point \((u,v)=\big(\frac{\pi}{2},0\big)\text{.}\)
