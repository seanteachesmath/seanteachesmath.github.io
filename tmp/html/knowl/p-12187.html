We'll sketch this surface using much the same procedure as we used in Examples 9.7.1 and 9.7.2. We'll only sketch the part of the surface in the first octant. The remaining parts (in the octants with \(x,y \lt 0\text{,}\) \(z\ge 0\text{,}\) with \(x,z \lt 0\text{,}\) \(y\ge 0\) and with \(y,z \lt 0\text{,}\) \(x\ge0\)) are just reflections of the first octant part.
