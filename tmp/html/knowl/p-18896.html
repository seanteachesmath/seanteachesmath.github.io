(a) \(\int_C \vnabla f\cdot \dee{\vr}=0\) is the work done along the curve using the conservative force \(\vnabla f\text{.}\) That work is difference between the potential \(f\) at the final point minus the potential \(f\) at the initial point. If the final and initial points are both on the level surface \(f(x,y,z)=0\text{,}\) that difference is zero.
