Theorem 15.1.4. Divergence Identities.
  1. \(\displaystyle \color{red}{\vnabla\cdot(\vF+\vG)=\vnabla\cdot\vF+\vnabla \cdot\vG}\)
  2. \(\color{red}{\vnabla\cdot(c\vF)=c\,\vnabla\cdot\vF}\text{,}\) for any constant \(c\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \color{red}{\vnabla\cdot(f\vF)=(\vnabla f)\cdot\vF+ f\,\vnabla\cdot\vF}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \vnabla\cdot(\vF\times\vG)=(\vnabla\times\vF)\cdot\vG - \vF\cdot(\vnabla\times\vG)\)
