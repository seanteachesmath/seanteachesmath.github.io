(c) True. If \(\vnabla f = 0\text{,}\) then

\begin{alignat*}{5} \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x,y,z)&=0 &&\quad\implies\quad & f(x,y,z)&=g(y,z)\\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x,y,z)&=0 &&\quad\implies\quad & \frac{\partial g}{\partial y}(y,z)&=0 &&\quad\implies\quad & g(y,z)&=h(z)\\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}(x,y,z)&=0 &&\quad\implies\quad & h'(z)&=0 &&\quad\implies\quad & h(z)&=C \end{alignat*}

for some functions \(g(y,z)\text{,}\) \(h(z)\) and constant \(C\text{.}\)
