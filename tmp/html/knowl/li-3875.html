Item.
In order for book #3 to not topple off of book #2, we need the centre of mass of book #3 to lie above book #2. That is, we need the \(x\)-coordinate of the centre mass of \(B_3\text{,}\) which is \(\bar X(B_3)=x_3-\frac{L}{2}\text{,}\) to obey
\begin{equation*} \bar X(B_3) \le x_2 \iff x_3-\frac{L}{2} \le x_2 \iff x_3\le x_2+\frac{L}{2} \end{equation*}
