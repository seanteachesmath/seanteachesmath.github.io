Solution 1: Let's see whether the divergence test works here.

\begin{equation*} \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{n^4 2^{n/3}}{(2n+7)^4}\left(\frac{\frac{1}{n^4}}{\frac{1}{n^4}}\right) = \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{2^{n/3}}{(2+7/n)^4} = \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{2^{n/3}}{(2+0)^4} = \infty \end{equation*}

The summands of our series do not converge to zero. By the divergence test, the series diverges.
