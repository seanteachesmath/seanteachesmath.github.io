The second derivative of \(f(x)\) is positive for \(x \gt \root 3\of 20\) and negative for \(x \lt \root 3\of 20\text{.}\) So the curve is concave up for \(x \gt \root 3\of 20\) and concave down for \(x \lt \root 3\of 20\text{.}\) There is an inflection point at \(x=\root 3\of 20\approx 2.7\text{,}\) \(y=\frac{18}{20^{4/3}}\approx 0.3\text{.}\)
