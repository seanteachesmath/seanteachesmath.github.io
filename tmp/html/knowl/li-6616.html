Item.

If \(0 \lt \veps \lt 1\text{,}\) completing the square in (C) gives

\begin{equation*} (1-\veps^2)\Big(x+\frac{\veps\ell}{1-\veps^2}\Big)^2 + y^2 = \ell^2 + \frac{\veps^2\ell^2}{1-\veps^2} = \frac{\ell^2}{1-\veps^2} \end{equation*}

which is equivalent to

\begin{equation*} \frac{\big(x+\frac{\veps\ell}{1-\veps^2}\big)^2} { \frac{\ell^2}{{(1-\veps^2)}^2}} +\frac{y^2}{\frac{\ell^2}{1-\veps^2}} =1 \end{equation*}

and is of course an ellipse with semi-major axis \(r_M=\frac{\ell}{1-\veps^2}\) and semi-minor axis \(r_m=\frac{\ell}{\sqrt{1-\veps^2}}\text{.}\)
