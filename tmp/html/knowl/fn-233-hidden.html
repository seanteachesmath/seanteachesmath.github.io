If you have not heard of “completing the square” don't worry. It is not a difficult method and it will only take you a few moments to learn. It refers to rewriting a quadratic polynomial
\begin{align*} P(x) &= ax^2 + bx + c &\text{as}&& P(x)= a(x+d)^2 +e \end{align*}
for new constants \(d,e\text{.}\)
