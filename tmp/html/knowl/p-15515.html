\begin{equation*} \vr(t) = e^t (\cos t, \sin t) \end{equation*}

parametrized in terms of arclength is

\begin{equation*} \vR(s)=\frac{s}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\cos\Big(\ln\Big(\frac{s}{\sqrt{2}}\Big)\Big)\,,\, \sin\Big(\ln\Big(\frac{s}{\sqrt{2}}\Big)\Big)\right). \end{equation*}
