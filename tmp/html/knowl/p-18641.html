(h) By the definition of arclength parametrisation, the arclength along the curve between \(\vr(0)\) and \(\vr(s)\) is \(s\text{.}\) In particular, the arclength between \(\vr(0)\) and \(\vr(3)\) is \(3\) and the arclength between \(\vr(0)\) and \(\vr(5)\text{,}\) which is the same as the arclength between \(\vr(0)\) and \(\vr(3)\) plus the arclength between \(\vr(3)\) and \(\vr(5)\text{,}\) is \(5\text{.}\) So the arclength between \(\vr(3)\) and \(\vr(5)\) is \(5-3=2\text{.}\)
