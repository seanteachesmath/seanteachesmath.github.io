(c) Call the constant vector \(\vv\times\vr\) of part (b) \(\vN\text{.}\) This vector is a constant and is perpendicular to both \(\vv(t)\) and \(\vr(t)\text{.}\) In particular

\begin{equation*} \vN\cdot\vr(t)=0 \end{equation*}

Assuming that \(\vN\) is nonzero, this is the equation of the plane through the origin with normal vector \(\vN\text{.}\)
