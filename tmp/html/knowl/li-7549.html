Item c.
The \(x\)-axis, \(\cX=\big\{\ (x,y)\in\bbbr^2\ \big|\ y=0\ \big\}\text{,}\) in \(\bbbr^2\) is not an open subset of \(\bbbr^2\) because for any point \((x_0,0)\in\cX\) and any \(\veps \gt 0\text{,}\) the ball \(B_\veps\big((x_0,0)\big)\) contains points with nonzero \(y\)-coordinates and so is not contained in \(\cX\text{.}\)
