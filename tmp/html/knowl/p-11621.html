A prism has the six vertices

\begin{alignat*}{2} A&=(1,0,0)\qquad & A'&=(5,0,1)\\ B&=(0,3,0) & B'&=(4,3,1)\\ C&=(0,0,4) & C'&=(4,0,5) \end{alignat*}
  1. Verify that three of the faces are parallelograms. Are they rectangular?
  2. Find the length of \(AA'\text{.}\)
  3. Find the area of the triangle \(ABC\text{.}\)
  4. Find the volume of the prism.
