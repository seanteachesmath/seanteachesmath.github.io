We have already computed \(\diff{\hat\vT}{s}\) and \(\diff{\hat\vB}{s}\text{.}\) It is now an easy matter to compute

\begin{align*} \diff{\hat\vN}{s} &= \diff{}{s}\big(\hat\vB(s)\times\hat\vT(s)\big)\\ &= -\tau(s)\hat\vN(s)\times\hat\vT(s) +\hat\vB(s)\times\big(\ka(s)\hat\vN(s)\big)\\ &=\tau(s)\hat\vB(s)-\ka(s)\hat\vT(s) \end{align*}

To see that \(\hat\vN(s)\times\hat\vT(s)=-\hat\vB(s)\) and \(\hat\vB(s)\times\hat\vN(s)=-\hat\vT(s)\text{,}\) just look at the right hand figure above.
