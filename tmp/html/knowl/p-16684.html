Consider the vector field

\begin{equation*} \vF(x, y, z) = -2y \cos x \sin x\,\hi + (\cos^2 x + (1 + yz) e^{yz} )\,\hj + y^2 e^{yz}\,\hk \end{equation*}
  1. Find a real valued function \(f (x, y, z)\) such that \(\vF = \vnabla f\text{.}\)
  2. Evaluate the line integral
    \begin{equation*} \int_C \vF \cdot \dee{\vr} \end{equation*}
    where \(C\) is the arc of the curve \(\vr(t) = \big(t, e^t , t^2 - \pi^2\big)\) , \(0 \le t \le \pi\text{,}\) traversed from \((0, 1, -\pi^2 )\) to \((\pi, e^\pi , 0)\text{.}\)
