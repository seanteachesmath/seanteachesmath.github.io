Item c.
Consider the surface given implicitly by the equation \(G(x,y,z) = K\text{,}\) where \(K\) is a constant. Let \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\) be a point on the surface and assume that the gradient \(\vnabla G\big(x_0,y_0,z_0\big)\ne \vZero\text{.}\) Then
\begin{gather*} \vn= \vnabla G\big(x_0,y_0,z_0\big) \end{gather*}
is normal to the surface at \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\text{.}\)
