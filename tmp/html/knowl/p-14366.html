On the domain of integration

  • \(x\) runs for \(0\) to \(2\text{,}\) and
  • for each fixed \(x\) in that range, \(y\) runs from \(0\) to \(\sqrt{4-x^2}\text{.}\) The equation \(y=\sqrt{4-x^2}\) is equivalent to \(x^2+y^2=4\text{,}\) \(y\ge 0\text{.}\)
