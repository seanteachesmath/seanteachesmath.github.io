Item b.
Given \(\vr(s)\text{,}\) i.e. if we have a parametrization of the curve in terms of arc length, then
\begin{equation*} \diff{\hat\vT}{s}(s) = \ka(s)\,\hat\vN(s) \end{equation*}
where \(\hat\vN(s)\) is the unit normal vector to the curve at \(\vr(s)\) that points toward the centre of curvature.
