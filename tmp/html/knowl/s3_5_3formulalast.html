Exercise 13. 2010H.

Consider an open-top rectangular baking pan with base dimensions \(x\) centimetres by \(y\) centimetres and height \(z\) centimetres that is made from \(A\) square centimetres of tin plate. Suppose \(y = px\) for some fixed constant \(p\text{.}\)

  1. Find the dimensions of the baking pan with the maximum capacity (i.e., maximum volume). Prove that your answer yields the baking pan with maximum capacity. Your answer will depend on the value of \(p\text{.}\)
  2. Find the value of the constant \(p\) that yields the baking pan with maximum capacity and give the dimensions of the resulting baking pan. Prove that your answer yields the baking pan with maximum capacity.
