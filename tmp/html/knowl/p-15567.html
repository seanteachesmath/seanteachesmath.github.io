For each \(s\text{,}\) \(\hat\vT(s)\text{,}\) \(\hat\vN(s)\) and \(\hat\vB(s)\) are mutually perpendicular unit vectors. They form an orthonormal basis for \(\bbbr^3\text{,}\) just as \(\hi\text{,}\) \(\hj\) and \(\hk\) form an orthonormal basis for \(\bbbr^3\text{.}\) Furthermore both \((\hat\vT(s)\,,\,\hat\vN(s)\,,\,\hat\vB(s))\) and \((\hi\,,\,\hj\,,\,\hk)\) are “right handed triples” 3 , meaning that \(\hat\vB(s) = \hat\vT(s)\times\hat\vN(s)\) and \(\hk=\hi\times\hj\text{.}\)
