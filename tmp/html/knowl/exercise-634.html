  2. Determine all of the following if they are present:

    1. \(x\)--coordinates of local maxima and minima, intervals where \(f(x)\) is increasing or decreasing;
    2. intervals where \(f(x)\) is concave upwards or downwards;
    3. equations of any horizontal or vertical asymptotes.
  3. Sketch the graph of \(y = f(x)\text{,}\) giving the \((x, y)\) coordinates for all points of interest above.
