Item.
Since \(C\) is contained in \(S_2\text{,}\) the tangent line to \(C\) at \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\) is contained in the tangent plane to \(S_2\) at \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\text{,}\) by Q[10.5.3.2]. In particular, any tangent vector, \(\vt\text{,}\) to \(C\) at \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\) must be perpendicular to \(-g_x(x_0,y_0)\,\hi -g_y(x_0,y_0)\,\hj+\hk\text{,}\) the normal vector to \(S_2\) at \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\text{.}\)
