Note: \(\ds\lim_{x \to \pm\infty}f(x)=0\text{.}\)

  1. Find all inflection points and intervals of increase, decrease, convexity up, and convexity down. You may use without proof the formula \(f''(x) = (x^3-3x)e^{-x^2/2}\text{.}\)
  2. Find local and global minima and maxima.
  3. Use all the above to draw a graph for \(f\text{.}\) Indicate all special points on the graph.
