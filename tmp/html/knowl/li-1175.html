Item.
When \(1\leq c\leq2\text{,}\) we know that \(18 \leq 22-c^2 \leq 21\text{,}\) and that numerator and denominator are non-negative, so
\begin{align*} \left|\frac{c}{6(22-c^2)}\right| &=\frac{c}{6(22-c^2)} \leq \frac{2}{6(22-c^2)} \leq \frac{2}{6\cdot 18}\\ & = \frac{1}{54} \leq \frac{1}{50} \end{align*}
as required.
