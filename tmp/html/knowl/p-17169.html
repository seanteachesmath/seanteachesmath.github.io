(a) The surface is \(g(x,y,z)=x^2+y^2+z^2-a^2=0\text{.}\) So, on the surface of the sphere,

\begin{align*} &\hn =\frac{\vnabla g}{|\vnabla g|}=\frac{x\hi+y\hj+z\hk}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}}\\ &\implies\ \vF\cdot\hn = \big(x^2+y^2+z^2\big)^{n++1-1/2} ={\big(a^2\big)}^{n+1/2}=a^{2n+1}\\ &\implies\ \dblInt_S\vF\cdot\hn \,\dee{S}\ =a^{2n+1}\dblInt_S\,\dee{S} =a^{2n+1}\text{Area}(S) =4\pi a^{2n+3} \end{align*}

since the surface area of a sphere of radius \(a\) is \(4\pi a^2\text{.}\)
