We want this in the form of the substitution rule, so we do a little massaging:
\begin{align*} \int_0^1 x^2\sin\big(x^3+1\big)\dee{x} &= \int_0^1 \frac{1}{3}\cdot 3x^2 \cdot \sin\big(x^3+1\big)\dee{x}\\ &= \frac{1}{3}\int_0^1 \sin\big(x^3+1\big)\cdot 3x^2 \dee{x}\\ &\hskip1.5in\text{by Theorem }\knowl{./knowl/thm_Intarith.html}{\text{6.2.1}}\text{(c)} \end{align*}
