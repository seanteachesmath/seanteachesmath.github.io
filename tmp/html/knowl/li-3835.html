Item.
If \(\ell' \gt N+1\text{,}\) with \(\ell'-N\) even,
\begin{align*} (-1)^N S_{N,\ell'}&=\overbrace{(A_{N+1}-A_{N+2})}^{\ge 0} +\overbrace{(A_{N+3}-A_{N+4})}^{\ge 0}+\cdots\\ \amp\hskip1in+\overbrace{(A_{\ell'-1}-A_{\ell'})}^{\ge 0}\\ \amp\ge 0\\ \\ \text{and}\\ (-1)^N S_{N,\ell'+1}&=\overbrace{(-1)^N S_{N,\ell'}}^{\ge 0} +\overbrace{A_{\ell'+1}}^{\ge 0} \ge 0 \end{align*}
This tells us that \((-1)^N S_{N,\ell}\ge 0\) for all \(\ell \gt N+1\text{,}\) both even and odd.
