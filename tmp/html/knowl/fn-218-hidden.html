Yet again what we really do is pick a natural number \(n\text{,}\) slice the octant of the sphere into \(n\) pancakes each of thickness \(\De x=\frac{r}{n}\) and then take the limit \(n\rightarrow\infty\text{.}\) In the integral \(\De x\) is replaced by \(dx\text{.}\) Knowing that this is what is going to happen, we again just skip a few steps.
