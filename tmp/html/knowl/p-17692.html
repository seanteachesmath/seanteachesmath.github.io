(d) Let's first figure out what the surface \(z^2 - x^2 - y^2 + 1 = 0\text{,}\) i.e. the surface \(x^2 + y^2 = 1 +z^2\text{,}\) looks like. For each \(z_0\text{,}\) the \(z=z_0\) cross-section of this surface is the circle \(x^2+y^2=1+z_0^2\text{.}\) The radius of this circle is \(1\) when \(z_0=0\) and grows as \(|z_0|\) increases.
