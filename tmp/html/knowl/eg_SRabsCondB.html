Example 8.4.4. \(\sum_{n=1}^\infty(-1)^{n-1}\frac{1}{n^2}\).

Because the series \(\sum_{n=1}^\infty\big|(-1)^{n-1}\frac{1}{n^2}\big| =\sum\limits_{n=1}^\infty\frac{1}{n^2}\) of Example 8.3.6 converges (by the integral test), the series \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^\infty(-1)^{n-1}\frac{1}{n^2}\) converges absolutely, and hence converges.
