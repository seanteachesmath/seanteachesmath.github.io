Let \(\vF = 6x^2yz^2\,\hi + (2x^3z^2 + 2y - xz)\,\hj + 4x^3yz\,\hk\) and let \(\vG = yz\,\hi + xy\,\hk\text{.}\)

  1. For what value of the constant \(\la\) is the vector field \(\vH = \vF + \la\vG\) conservative on 3-space?
  2. Find a scalar potential \(\phi(x,y,z)\) for the conservative field \(\vH\) referred to in part (a).
  3. Find \(\int_C\vF\cdot \dee{\vr}\) if \(C\) is the curve of intersection of the two surfaces \(z = x\) and \(y = e^{xz}\) from the point \((0, 1, 0)\) to the point \((1, e, 1)\text{.}\)
