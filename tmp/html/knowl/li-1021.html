The volume of a sphere  17  of radius \(r\) is \(V(r)=\frac{4}{3}\pi r^3\text{.}\) The error in the volume computed with the measured radius is
\begin{align*} \De V &=V(r_0+\De r)-V(r_0)\approx V'(r_0)\De r\\ &= 4\pi r_0^2 \Delta r \end{align*}
where we have again made use of the linear approximation, equation 4.4.20.
