Item.
The third series is
\begin{equation*} \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n\qquad\text{with }a_n = \frac{1}{n^2} \end{equation*}
We have already seen, in Example 8.3.6 with \(p=2\text{,}\) that this series converges. But it also has
\begin{equation*} \Big|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\Big| = \bigg|\frac{\frac{1}{(n+1)^2}}{\frac{1}{n^2}}\bigg| = \frac{n^2}{(n+1)^2} = \frac{1}{(1+\frac{1}{n})^2} \rightarrow L=1\quad\text{as $n\rightarrow\infty$} \end{equation*}
