Setting our two expressions for \(|\va-\vb|^2\) equal to each other,

\begin{gather*} |\va-\vb|^2=|\va|^2+|\vb|^2-2\va\cdot\vb =|\vb|^2-2|\va|\,|\vb|\,\cos\theta+|\va|^2 \end{gather*}

cancelling the \(|\va|^2\) and \(|\vb|^2\) common to both sides

\begin{gather*} -2\va\cdot\vb =-2|\va|\,|\vb|\,\cos\theta \end{gather*}

and dividing by \(-2\) gives

\begin{gather*} \va\cdot\vb=|\va|\,|\vb|\,\cos\theta \end{gather*}

which is exactly property 6.
