The \(N\)th term of the sequence of partial sums, \(S_N\text{,}\) is the sum of the first \(N\) terms of the series \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n}\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{l|l} \mathbf{N}&\mathbf{S_N}\\ \hline 1 & 1\\ 2 & 1+\frac12\\ 3 & 1+\frac12 + \frac13\\ 4 & 1+\frac12 + \frac13+\frac14\\ 5 & 1+\frac12 + \frac13+\frac14+\frac15\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
