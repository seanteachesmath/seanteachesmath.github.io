This integral is a little different than other integrals that we have encountered so far in that the integrand is a vector. By definition 10 ,

\begin{equation*} \dblInt_S \big[G_1\,\hi + G_2\,\hj + G_3\,\hk \big]\ \dee{S} =\hi \dblInt_S G_1\ \dee{S} +\hj \dblInt_S G_2\ \dee{S} +\hk \dblInt_S G_3\ \dee{S} \end{equation*}

so we just have to compute the three components separately.
