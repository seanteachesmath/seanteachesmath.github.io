Item 16.2.11.i.
Let \(\vF\) be a vector field and suppose that \(S_1\) and \(S_2\) are oriented surfaces with the same boundary curve \(C\text{,}\) and \(C\) is given the direction that is compatible with the orientations of \(S_1\) and \(S_2\) . Then \(\dblInt_{S 1} \vF\cdot\hn\, \dee{S} = \dblInt_{S 2} \vF\cdot\hn\, \dee{S}\text{.}\)
