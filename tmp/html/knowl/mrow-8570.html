\begin{align*} \bar x &= \frac{\int_{-3}^{10} x\cdot \frac{1}{1+x^2}\,\dee{x}}{\int_{-3}^{10} \frac{1}{1+x^2}\,\dee{x}}\\ \end{align*}

For the numerator, we use the substitution \(u=1+x^2\text{,}\) \(\dee{u}=2x\,\dee{x}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} &= \frac{\frac{1}{2}\int_{10}^{101} \frac{1}{u}\,\dee{u}}{\Big[\arctan x \Big]_{-3}^{10} } =\frac{\frac{1}{2}\Big[\log u\Big]_{10}^{101}}{\arctan 10 - \arctan(-3)}\\ &=\frac{\Big[\log 101-\log 10\Big]}{2(\arctan 10 + \arctan(3))}=\frac{\log 10.1}{2(\arctan 10 + \arctan(3))}\approx 0.43 \end{align*}
