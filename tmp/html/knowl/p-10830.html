There is another way to prove that the series \(\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{x^n}{n!}\) converges to the function \(e^x\text{.}\) Rather than looking at how the error term \(E_n(x)\) behaves as \(n \to \infty\text{,}\) we can show that the series satisfies the same simple differential equation  6  and the same initial condition as the function.
