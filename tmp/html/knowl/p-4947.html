(a) You might not be familiar with hyperbolic sine and cosine, but you don't need to be. We can graph them using the same methods as the other curves in this section. The derivatives are given to us:

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\{\sinh x\}\amp=\cosh x =\frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2}\\ \diff{}{x}\{\cosh x\}\amp=\sinh x = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2}\\ \left(\diff{}{x}\right)^2\{\sinh x\}\amp=\sinh x =\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2}\\ \left(\diff{}{x}\right)^2\{\cosh x\}\amp=\cosh x = \frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2} \end{align*}
