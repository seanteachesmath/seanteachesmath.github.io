Equation 15.1.24.

If a fluid has velocity field \(\vv\) and you place an infinitesimal paddlewheel at \(\vr_0\) with its axle in direction \(\vn\text{,}\) then it rotates at \(\frac{1}{2}\vnabla\times \vv(\vr_0)\cdot \hn\) radians per unit time. In particular, to maximize the rate of rotation, orient the paddlewheel so that \(\hn\parallel\vnabla\times \vv(\vr_0)\text{.}\)
