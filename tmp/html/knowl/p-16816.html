A particle of mass

\begin{equation*} m=2 \end{equation*}

is acted on by a force

\begin{equation*} \vF = \big(4t\,,\,6t^2\,,\,-4t\big) \end{equation*}

At \(t=0\text{,}\) the particle has velocity zero and is located at the point \((1,2,3)\text{.}\)

  1. Find the velocity vector \(\vv(t)\) for \(t\ge 0\text{.}\)
  2. Find the position vector \(\vr(t)\) for \(t\ge 0\text{.}\)
  3. Find \(\ka(t)\) the curvature of the path traversed by the particle for \(t\ge 0\text{.}\)
  4. Find the work done by the force on the particle from \(t=0\) to \(t=T\text{.}\)
