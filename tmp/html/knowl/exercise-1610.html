Let \(A\) be some constant and let \(G(\gamma, s, t) = F (\gamma + s, \gamma-s, At)\text{.}\) Find the value of \(A\) such that \(\frac{\partial G}{\partial t} = \frac{\partial^2 G}{\partial \gamma^2} + \frac{\partial^2 G}{\partial s^2}\text{.}\)
