Item 12.4.1.25.c.
Find the osculating plane to \(C\) at the point \(\big(1/\sqrt{2}\,,1/\sqrt{2}\,,\,0\big)\text{.}\) In general, the osculating plane to a curve \(\vr(t)\) at the point \(\vr(t_0)\) is the plane which fits the curve best at \(\vr(t_0)\text{.}\) It passes through \(\vr(t_0)\) and has normal vector \(\hat\vB(t_0)\text{.}\)
