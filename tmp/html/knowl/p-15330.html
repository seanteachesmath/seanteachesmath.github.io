(a) We can parametrize the circle \(\ x^2+y^2=9\ \) as \(x(\theta)=3\cos\theta\text{,}\) \(y(\theta)=3\sin\theta\) with \(\theta\) running from \(0\) to \(2\pi\text{.}\) As \(z=2x+3y\text{,}\) the ellipse can be parametrized by

\begin{align*} &x(\theta)=3\cos\theta,\ y(\theta)=3\sin\theta,\ z(\theta)=2x(\theta)+3 y(\theta) =6\cos\theta+9\sin\theta\\ & 0\le\theta\le 2\pi \end{align*}
