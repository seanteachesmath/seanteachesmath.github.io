Doing things formally with \(\epsilon\)'s and \(\delta\)'s is quite painful for general functions. It is far better to make use of the arithmetic of limits (Theorem 2.4.3) and some basic building blocks (like those in Theorem 2.4.1). Thankfully for most of the problems we deal with in calculus (at this level at least) can be approached in exactly this way.
