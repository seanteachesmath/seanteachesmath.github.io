\begin{align*} 2n\sin\left(\frac{1}{n}\right)&=2\left(\frac{\sin x}{x}\right)\\ \end{align*}

If \(n \to \infty\text{,}\) then \(x = \frac{1}{n}\to 0\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \lim_{n \to \infty}2n\sin\left(\frac{1}{n}\right)&=2\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}\\ \end{align*}

That limit is familiar:

\begin{align*} &=2(1)=2\\ \end{align*}

Then:

\begin{align*} \lim_{n \to \infty}\log\left(2n\sin\left(\frac{1}{n}\right)\right)&=\log 2 \end{align*}
