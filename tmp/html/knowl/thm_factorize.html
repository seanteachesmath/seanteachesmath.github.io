Theorem 11.1.7.

If the domain of integration

\begin{equation*} \cR=\Set{(x,y)}{a\le x\le b,\ c\le y\le d} \end{equation*}

is a rectangle and the integrand is the product \(f(x,y)=g(x)h(y)\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation*} \dblInt_\cR f(x,y)\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =\left[ \int_a^b\dee{x}\ g(x)\right]\ \left[\int_c^d\dee{y}\ h(y)\right] \end{equation*}
