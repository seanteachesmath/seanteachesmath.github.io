(a) Since \(-1\le \sin(4\theta)\le 1\text{,}\) the coordinate \(r=2+\sin(4\theta)\) oscillates between \(r=1\) and \(r=3\) as \(\theta\) runs from \(0\) to \(2\pi\text{.}\) The maximum value \(r=3\) is achieved when \(\sin(4\theta)=1\text{,}\) i.e when \(4\theta=\frac{\pi}{2} +2n\pi\text{,}\) i.e. when \(\theta=\frac{\pi}{8}+\frac{n\pi}{2}\text{.}\) That matches figure (E).
