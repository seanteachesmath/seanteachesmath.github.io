Our parametrization is

\begin{align*} x(u,v) &= u+v\\ y(u,v) &= u^2+v^2\\ z(u,v) &= u-v \end{align*}
  • Adding \(x(u,v)\) and \(z(u,v)\) gives \(x(u,v)+z(u,v) = 2u\text{.}\)
  • Subtracting \(z(u,v)\) from \(x(u,v)\) gives \(x(u,v)-z(u,v) = 2v\text{.}\)

So \(u=\frac{1}{2}\big(x(u,v)+z(u,v)\big)\) and \(v=\frac{1}{2}\big(x(u,v)-z(u,v)\big)\text{.}\) So on our surface

\begin{align*} y(u,v) &= u^2+v^2 = \frac{1}{4}\big(x(u,v)+z(u,v)\big)^2 +\frac{1}{4}\big(x(u,v)-z(u,v)\big)^2\\ &= \frac{1}{2} x(u,v)^2 + \frac{1}{2} z(u,v)^2 \end{align*}

All points of our surface lie on \(2y= x^2+z^2\text{.}\) This is a parabolic bowl:

  • no points have \(y \lt 0\) and
  • the \(y=Y\) (with \(Y \gt 0\)) cross-section is the circle \(x^2+z^2=2Y\text{,}\) \(y=Y\)
  • the \(x=0\) cross-section is the parabola \(2y=z^2\text{,}\) \(x=0\)
  • the \(z=0\) cross-section is the parabola \(2y=x^2\text{,}\) \(z=0\)
