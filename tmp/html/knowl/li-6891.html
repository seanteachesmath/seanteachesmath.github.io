Item 13.4.2.34.b.
\(\int_C \vF \cdot\hn\, \dee{s}\text{,}\) where \(\vF(x, y) = 2x^2\hi + ye^x\hj\text{,}\) \(C\) is the boundary of the square \(0 \le x \le 1\text{,}\) \(0 \le y \le 1\text{.}\) Here \(\hn\) is the unit normal vector pointing outward from the square, and \(s\) is arc length.
