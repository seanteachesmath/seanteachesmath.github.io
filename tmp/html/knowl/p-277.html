We write this as

\begin{gather*} \lim_{x\to 0} \sin \left(\frac{\pi}{x}\right) \text{ does not exist} \end{gather*}

It's not very inventive notation, however it is clear. We frequently abbreviate “does not exist” to “DNE” and rewrite the above as

\begin{align*} \lim_{x\to 0} \sin \left(\frac{\pi}{x}\right) &= \text{DNE} \end{align*}
