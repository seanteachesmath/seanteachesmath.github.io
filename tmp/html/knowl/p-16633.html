We'd like a single function \(\varphi(x,y,z)\) that simultaneously obeys all three of these equations, for some \(\psi_j\)'s. An initial guess is simply the sum of all of the distinct terms, other that the \(\psi_j\)'s, that appear in the three equations above. The term \(xe^y\) appears in the \(\psi_1\) and \(\psi_2\) equations and the term \(\be y\cos z\) appears in the \(\psi_2\) and \(\psi_3\) equations. So we guess

\begin{equation*} \varphi(x,y,z)\stackrel{?}{=} xe^y+\beta y \cos z \end{equation*}
