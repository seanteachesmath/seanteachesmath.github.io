Let's try a family of polygonal paths \(C_Y\) that consist of

  • the line segment \(L_1\) from \((2,2)\) to \((2,Y)\) followed by
  • the line segment \(L_2\) from \((2,Y)\) to \((1,Y)\) followed by
  • the line segment \(L_3\) from \((1,Y)\) to \((1,1)\text{.}\)

This is a way of characterizing a family of alternate paths with only one parameter, \(Y\text{.}\) We are hoping that the value of the integral \(\int_{C_Y} \vF\cdot\dee{\vr}\) depends on \(Y\) and that we can choose a specific value of \(Y\) so as to make the value of the integral \(\int_{C_Y} \vF\cdot\dee{\vr}\) exactly \(4\text{.}\)
