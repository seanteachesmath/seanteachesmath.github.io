In terms of our original vector field, this condition is that \(\frac{\partial \vv_i}{\partial x_j}\big(\vx_0,t_0\big) =\frac{\partial \vv_j}{\partial x_i}\big(\vx_0,t_0\big)\text{.}\) So, in three dimensions, it comes down to the requirement that \(\vnabla\times\vv\) be zero at the point \(\big(\vx_0,t_0\big)\text{.}\)
