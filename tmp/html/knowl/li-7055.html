Item.
Place a train in the tunnel at \(V\text{.}\) Assume that the only forces acting on the train are gravity, \(\vG\text{,}\) and a normal force, \(\vN\text{,}\) that the tunnel imposes on the train to keep it in the tunnel. In particular, there are no frictional forces, like air resistance, and the train does not have an engine. Release the train and assume that it does not melt as it passes through the centre of the Earth.
