(a) We are to compute the divergence of \(x^2 y\,\hi + e^y \sin x\,\hj + e^{zx}\,\hk\text{.}\) Since

\begin{alignat*}{1} \frac{\partial }{\partial x}\big(x^2 y\big) &=2xy\\ \frac{\partial }{\partial y}\big(e^y \sin x\big) &= e^y\sin x\\ \frac{\partial }{\partial z}\big(e^{zx}\big) &=xe^{xz} \end{alignat*}

the specified divergence is

\begin{align*} \vnabla\cdot\big(x^2 y\,\hi + e^y \sin x\,\hj + e^{zx}\,\hk\big) &= 2xy + e^y\sin x + xe^{xz} \end{align*}
