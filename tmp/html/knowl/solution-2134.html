Solution 13.3.1.13.1.

Following ExampleÂ 13.3.3, the particle can never escape the region \(\{(x,y,z) : \varphi(x,y,z)\ge -E\}\text{.}\) So, we should find \(E\text{,}\) then figure out the region.

The kinetic energy of the particle is \(\frac{1}{2}m|\vv|^2\text{,}\) so the total energy of the system (also the kinetic energy when the potential energy is 0) is \(\frac{1}{2}(10)(2^2)=20\) J.

Therefore, a region it can never escape is

\begin{equation*} \Set{(x,y,z)}{\varphi(x,y,z)\ge -20} \end{equation*}

that is,

\begin{equation*} \Set{(x,y,z)}{x^2+y^2+z^2 \le 20} \end{equation*}

So, it can never escape the sphere centred at the origin with radius \(\sqrt{20}\text{.}\)
