Solution 5.1.2.10.1.

We note that \(f(x)\) looks similar to \(\dfrac{1}{x}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\left\{\log|x|\right\}&=\frac{1}{x}\\ \diff{}{x}\left\{\log|x+2|\right\}&=\frac{1}{x+2} \end{align*}

The most general antiderivative of \(f(x)\) is \(F(x)=\log|x+2|+C\text{.}\)
