Use cylindrical coordinates to evaluate the volumes of each of the following regions.

  1. Above the \(xy\)--plane, inside the cone \(z=2a-\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\) and inside the cylinder \(x^2+y^2=2ay\text{.}\)
  2. Above the \(xy\)--plane, under the paraboloid \(z=1-x^2-y^2\) and in the wedge \(-x\le y\le \sqrt{3}x\text{.}\)
  3. Above the paraboloid \(\ z=x^2+y^2\ \) and below the plane \(\ z=2y\text{.}\)
