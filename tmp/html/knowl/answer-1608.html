Answer 10.4.5.15.1.

(a)

\begin{align*} \pdiff{z}{t}(r,t) &= -r\sin t\ \pdiff{f}{x}(r\cos t\,,\,r\sin t) +r\cos t\ \pdiff{f}{y}(r\cos t\,,\,r\sin t) \end{align*}

(b)

\begin{align*} \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial t^2}(r,t) &= -r\cos t \ \pdiff{f}{x} -r\sin t \ \pdiff{f}{y}\\ &\hskip0.5in +r^2\sin^2 t\ \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} -2r^2\sin t\cos t\ \frac{\partial^2\ f}{\partial x\partial y} +r^2\cos^2 t\ \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} \end{align*}

with all of the partial derivatives of \(f\) evaluated at \((r\cos t\,,\,r\sin t)\text{.}\)
