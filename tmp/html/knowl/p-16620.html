Evaluate the line integral \(\int_C \vF\cdot \dee{\vr}\text{,}\) where \(\vF\) is the conservative vector field

\begin{equation*} \vF (x, y, z) = \big(y + ze^x , x + e^y \sin z, z + e^x + e^y \cos z\big) \end{equation*}

and \(C\) is the curve given by the parametrization

\begin{equation*} \vr(t) = (t, e^t , \sin t),\qquad t \text{ from } 0 \text{ to } \pi. \end{equation*}
