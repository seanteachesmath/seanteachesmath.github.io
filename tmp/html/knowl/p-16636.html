Consider the vector field \(\vF(x,y,z) = (\cos x, 2 + \sin y, e^z)\text{.}\)

  1. Compute the curl of \(\vF\text{.}\)
  2. Is there a function \(f\) such that \(\vF = \vnabla f\text{?}\) Justify your answer.
  3. Compute the integral \(\int_C \vF\cdot\dee{\vr}\) along the curve \(C\) parametrized by \(\vr(t) = (t, \cos t, \sin t)\) with \(0 \le t \le 3\pi\text{.}\)
