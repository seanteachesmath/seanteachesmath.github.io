Item.
As our leftmost strip is at \(x=0\) and our rightmost strip is at \(x=4\text{,}\) the total
\begin{align*} \text{Volume} &= \int _0^4 2\pi x^{3/2} \dee{x} =\left[\frac{4\pi}{5} x^{5/2} \right]_0^4 = \frac{4\pi}{5}\cdot 32 = \frac{128\pi}{5} \end{align*}
which (thankfully) agrees with our previous computation.
