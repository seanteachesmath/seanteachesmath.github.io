(d) At time \(\pi\text{,}\) the tack is at \(\vr(\pi)=(\pi,2)\text{,}\) which is at the top of its trajectory. Looking at the sketch in part (a), we see that, at that time \(\hN(\pi) = -\hj\text{.}\) So the osculating circle at time \(t=\pi\) has center

\begin{equation*} \vr(\pi) + \rho(\pi) \hN(\pi) =(\pi,2) + 4(0,-1) =(\pi,-2) \end{equation*}

and radius \(\rho(\pi)=4\text{.}\) So the equation of the osculating circle at time \(\pi\) is

\begin{equation*} (x-\pi)^2 +(y+2)^2 = 16 \end{equation*}
