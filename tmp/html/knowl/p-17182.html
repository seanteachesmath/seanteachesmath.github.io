Let \(G(x,y,z) = z-xy\text{.}\) Then, using (14.3.3),

\begin{align*} \hn\,\dee{S}&=\frac{\vnabla G}{\vnabla G\cdot\hk}\ \dee{x}\dee{y} =\frac{-y\hi-x\hj+\hk}{1}\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =(-y\hi-x\hj+\hk)\,\dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}

so that

\begin{align*} \frac{x^2y}{\sqrt{1+x^2+y^2}}\ \dee{S} &=\frac{x^2y}{\sqrt{1+x^2+y^2}}\ \sqrt{y^2+x^2+1}\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =x^2y\ \dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}

and

\begin{align*} \vF\cdot\hn\,\dee{S} &=\big[x\hi+y\hj+\hk\big]\cdot\big[-y\hi-x\hj+\hk\big]\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =\big[1-2xy\big]\,\dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}
