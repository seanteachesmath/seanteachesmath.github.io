Evaluate the line integrals below. (Use any method you like.)

  1. \(\int_C (x^2 + y)\,\dee{x} + x\,\dee{y}\text{,}\) where \(C\) is the arc of the parabola \(y = 9 - x^2\) from \((-3, 0)\) to \((3, 0)\text{.}\)
  2. \(\int_C \vF \cdot\hn\, \dee{s}\text{,}\) where \(\vF(x, y) = 2x^2\hi + ye^x\hj\text{,}\) \(C\) is the boundary of the square \(0 \le x \le 1\text{,}\) \(0 \le y \le 1\text{.}\) Here \(\hn\) is the unit normal vector pointing outward from the square, and \(s\) is arc length.
