Let \(G(x,y,z) = x^2+y^2+2z\text{.}\) Then, by (14.3.3),

\begin{align*} \hn\,\dee{S}&=\frac{\vnabla G}{\vnabla G\cdot\hk}\ \dee{x}\dee{y} =\frac{2x\hi+2y\hj+2\hk}{2}\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =(x\hi+y\hj+\hk)\,\dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}

so that

\begin{align*} \frac{x^2+y^2}{\sqrt{1+x^2+y^2}}\ \dee{S} &=\frac{x^2+y^2}{\sqrt{1+x^2+y^2}}\ \sqrt{1+x^2+y^2}\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =(x^2+y^2)\ \dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}

and

\begin{align*} \vF\cdot\hn\,\dee{S} &=\big[x\hi+y\hj+z\hk\big]\cdot\big[x\hi+y\hj+\hk\big]\,\dee{x}\dee{y} =\big[x^2+y^2+z\big]\,\dee{x}\dee{y}\\ &=\Big[1+\frac{1}{2}(x^2+y^2)\Big]\,\dee{x}\dee{y} \end{align*}

since \(z=1-\half(x^2+y^2)\) on \(\cS\text{.}\)
