Exercise 42. M317 2010A.

Let \(\sigma_1\) be the open surface given by \(z = 1 - x^2 - y^2\text{,}\) \(z \ge 0\text{.}\) Let \(\sigma_2\) be the open surface given by \(z = x^2 + y^2 - 1\text{,}\) \(z \le 0\text{.}\) Let \(\sigma_3\) be the planar surface given by \(z = 0\text{,}\) \(x^2 + y^2 \le 1\text{.}\) Let \(\vF = [ a ( y^2 + z^2 ) + bxz ]\,\hi + [ c ( x^2 + z^2 ) + dyz ]\,\hj + x^2\,\hk\) where \(a\text{,}\) \(b\text{,}\) \(c\text{,}\) and \(d\) are constants.

  1. Find the flux of \(\vF\) upwards across \(\sigma_1\text{.}\)
  2. Find all values of the constants \(a\text{,}\) \(b\text{,}\) \(c\text{,}\) and \(d\) so that the flux of \(\vF\) outwards across the closed surface \(\sigma_1 \cup \sigma_3\) is zero.
  3. Find all values of the constants \(a\text{,}\) \(b\text{,}\) \(c\text{,}\) and \(d\) so that the flux of \(\vF\) outwards across the closed surface \(\sigma_1 \cup \sigma_2\) is zero.
