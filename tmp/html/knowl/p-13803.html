We have to minimize \(f(x,y,z)=x^2+y^2+z^2\) subject to the constraint \(g(x,y,z) = 2(x+1)^2 + y^2 + 2(z-1)^2 -8\text{.}\) By Theorem 10.10.2, any local minimum or maximum \((x,y,z)\) must obey the Lagrange multiplier equations

\begin{align*} f_x = 2x &=4 \la (x+1) = \la g_x \tag{E1}\\ f_y = 2y &=2 \la y = \la g_y \tag{E2}\\ f_z = 2z &=4 \la (z-1) = \la g_z \tag{E3}\\ 2(x+1)^2 + y^2 + 2(z-1)^2 &= 8 \tag{E4} \end{align*}

for some real number \(\la\text{.}\)
