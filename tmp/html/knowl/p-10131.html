In this optional section we provide proofs of two convergence tests. We shall repeatedly use the fact that any sequence \(a_1\text{,}\) \(a_2\text{,}\) \(a_3\text{,}\) \(\cdots\text{,}\) of real numbers which is increasing (i.e. \(a_{n+1}\ge a_n\) for all \(n\)) and bounded (i.e. there is a constant \(M\) such that \(a_n\le M\) for all \(n\)) converges. We shall not prove this fact  20 .
