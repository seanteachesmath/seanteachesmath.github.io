The given surface is an ellipsoid centred at \((x,y,z)=(0,0,1)\text{.}\) It caps a curve \(\cC\) in the plane \(z=0\text{,}\) given by \(x^2+y^2=4\text{.}\) This is a circle of radius \(2\) centred at the origin, oriented counterclockwise when viewed from the positive \(z\)-axis.
