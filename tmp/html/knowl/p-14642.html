(a) The domain of integration is

\begin{align*} V &= \Set{(x,y,z)}{0\le z\le 1,\ 0\le y\le 1-z,\ 0\le x\le 1-z}\\ &= \Set{(x,y,z)}{ x,y,z\ge 0,\ x+z\le 1,\ y+z\le 1 } \end{align*}
