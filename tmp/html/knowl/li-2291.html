Since \(z\) runs from \(-h\) to \(+h\text{,}\) the total volume of wood in the napkin ring of radius \(R\) is
\begin{align*} \int_{-h}^h \pi(h^2-z^2)\dee{z} &=\pi\Big[h^2z-\frac{z^3}{3}\Big]_{-h}^h\\ &=\pi\Big[\Big(h^3-\frac{h^3}{3}\Big) -\Big((-h)^3-\frac{(-h)^3}{3}\Big)\Big]\\ &=\pi\Big[\frac{2}{3}h^3-\frac{2}{3}\big(-h\big)^3\Big]\\ &=\frac{4\pi}{3}h^3 \end{align*}
