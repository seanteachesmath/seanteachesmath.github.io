Step 1: Develop intuition. When \(n\gg 1\text{,}\) the numerator \(\sqrt{n+1}\approx \sqrt{n}\text{,}\) and the denominator \(n^2-2n+3\approx n^2\) so that \(a_n\approx \frac{\sqrt{n}}{n^2}=\frac{1}{n^{3/2}}\) and it looks like our series should converge by Example 8.3.6 with \(p=\frac{3}{2}\text{.}\)
