\begin{align*} \Delta y &\approx s'(4)\Delta x\\ \end{align*}

From the problem, \(\Delta x= 5-4=1\text{.}\) Differentiating,

\begin{align*} s'(t)&=\sqrt{19.6}\cdot\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}=\sqrt{\frac{4.9}{x}},\mbox{ so }\\ s'(4)&=\sqrt{\frac{4.9}{4}}\\ \end{align*}

The linear approximation gives us

\begin{align*} \Delta y& \approx \sqrt{\frac{4.9}{4}}(1)\approx1.1 \end{align*}
