The coordinates of the red dot are \(\big(e^Y\sin\theta\,,\,Y\,,\,e^Y\cos\theta\big)\text{.}\) This also gives a parametrization of the surface of revolution

\begin{align*} x(Y,\theta) & = e^Y\sin\theta\\ y(Y,\theta) & = Y\\ z(Y,\theta) & = e^Y\cos\theta\\ &0\le Y\le 1,\qquad 0\le\theta \lt 2\pi \end{align*}
