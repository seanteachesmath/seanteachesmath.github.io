Let \(S\) be the part of the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2=2\) where \(y\ge 1\text{,}\) oriented away from the origin.

  1. Compute
    \begin{equation*} \dblInt_S y^3\,\dee{S} \end{equation*}
  2. Compute
    \begin{equation*} \dblInt_S \big(xy\,\hi + xz\,\hj + zy\,\hk\big)\cdot\hn\,\dee{S} \end{equation*}
