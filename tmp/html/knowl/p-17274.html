(a) The specified surface is of the form

\begin{equation*} G(x,y,z) = x^2 + z^2 - \cos^2 y = 0 \end{equation*}

So one normal vector at the point \(\big(\frac{1}{2} , \frac{\pi}{4} , \frac{1}{2} \big)\) is

\begin{equation*} \vnabla G\left(\frac{1}{2} , \frac{\pi}{4} , \frac{1}{2} \right) =\big(2x \,,\, 2\sin y\cos y \,,\, 2z \big)\Big|_{ \big(\frac{1}{2} , \frac{\pi}{4} , \frac{1}{2} )} =(1,1,1) \end{equation*}

and an equation for the tangent plane at \(\big(\frac{1}{2} , \frac{\pi}{4} , \frac{1}{2} \big)\) is

\begin{gather*} (1,1,1)\cdot\big(x-1/2\,,\,y-\pi/4\,,\, z-1/2\big) =0\quad\text{or}\quad x+y+z = 1+\pi/4 \end{gather*}
