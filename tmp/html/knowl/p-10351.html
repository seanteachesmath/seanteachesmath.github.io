Solution 2: Let's develop some intuition for a comparison. For very large \(n\text{,}\) \(2n\) dominates \(7\) so that

\begin{gather*} \frac{n^4 2^{n/3}}{(2n+7)^4} \approx \frac{n^4 2^{n/3}}{(2n)^4} =\frac{1}{16}2^{n/3} \end{gather*}

The series \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} 2^{n/3}\) is a geometric series with ratio \(r=2^{1/3} \gt 1\) and so diverges. (It also fails the divergence test.) We expect the given series to diverge too.
