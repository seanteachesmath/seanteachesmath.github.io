Observe that in the definition of \(\alpha_\vF\text{,}\) we did not specify the radius \(\veps\) of the circle \(\cC_\veps\) to be used for the integration curve. That's because the answer to the integral does not depend on the choice of \(\veps\text{.}\) To see this, take any \(0 \lt \veps' \lt \veps \lt 1\) and consider the surface \(S=\big\{\ (x,y)\in\bbbr^2\ \big|\ \veps' \lt |(x,y)| \lt \veps\big\}\text{.}\)
