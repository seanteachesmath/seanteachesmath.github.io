Item c.
Applying part (b) with \(g(x)=M\) for all \(a\le x\le b\) gives
\begin{gather*} \int_a^b f(x)\,\dee{x} \le \int_a^b M\,\dee{x} = M(b-a) \end{gather*}
Similarly, viewing \(m\) as a (constant) function, and applying part (b) gives
\begin{gather*} m\le f(x) \implies \overbrace{\int_a^bm\,\dee{x}}^{=m(b-a)} \le \int_a^b f(x)\,\dee{x} \end{gather*}
