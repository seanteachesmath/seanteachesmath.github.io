Solution 8.2.2.9.1.

LemmaÂ 8.2.5 tells us \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^N ar^n = a\dfrac{1-r^{N+1}}{1-r}\text{,}\) for \(r \neq 1\text{.}\) Our geometric sum has \(a=1\text{,}\) \(r=\frac{1}{5}\text{,}\) and \(N=100\text{.}\) So:

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^{100}\frac{1}{5^n} = \frac{1-\frac{1}{5^{101}}}{1-\frac{1}{5}} = \dfrac{5^{101}-1}{4\cdot 5^{100}} \end{equation*}
