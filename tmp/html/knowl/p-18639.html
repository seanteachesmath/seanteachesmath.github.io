(f) The cube \(S\) has six sides. So the outward flux through \(\partial S\) is \(6\) and, by the divergence theorem,

\begin{align*} 6 &= \dblInt_{\partial S}\vF\cdot\hn\,\dee{S} =\tripInt_S \vnabla\cdot\vF\,\dee{V} = \tripInt_S C\,\dee{V} = C \end{align*}

since \(S\) has volume one. So \(C=6\text{.}\)
