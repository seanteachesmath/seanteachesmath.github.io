The uncovered area of the pipe can be broken up into a triangle (of height \(\textcolor{orange}{1-h}\) and base \(\textcolor{green}{2\sqrt{2h-h^2}}\)) and a sector of a circle (with angle \(2\pi-2\theta\)). The area of the triangle is

\begin{equation*} \underbrace{(1-h)}_{\mbox{height}}\underbrace{\sqrt{2h-h^2}}_{\frac{1}{2}\mbox{base}}. \end{equation*}

The area of the sector is

\begin{equation*} \underbrace{\left(\frac{2\pi-2\theta}{2\pi}\right)}_{\atp{\mbox{fraction}}{\mbox{of circle}}} \underbrace{(\pi\cdot 1^2)}_{ \atp{\mbox{area} }{\mbox{of circle} }} =\pi-\theta. \end{equation*}
