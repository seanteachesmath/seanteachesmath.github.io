Item 9.1.1.3.c.

\(x^2+y^2+z^2\) is the square of the distance from \((0,0,0)\) to \((x,y,z)\text{.}\) So \(x^2+y^2+z^2=4\) is the set of points whose distance from \((0,0,0)\) is \(2\text{.}\) It is the sphere with centre \((0,0,0)\) and radius 2.
