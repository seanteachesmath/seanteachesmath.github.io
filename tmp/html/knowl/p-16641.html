(a) The curl of \(\vF\) is

\begin{align*} \vnabla\times\vF &=\det\left[\begin{matrix} \hi &\hj &\hk\\ \pdiff{}{x} & \pdiff{}{y} & \pdiff{}{z}\\ \cos x & 2+\sin y & e^z \end{matrix} \right] =\vZero \end{align*}

Because \(F_1\) is a function only of \(x\text{,}\) \(F_2\) is a function only of \(y\text{,}\) and \(F_3\) is a function only of \(z\text{,}\) that all partial derivatives used in computing the curl are 0.
