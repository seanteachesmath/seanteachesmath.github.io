Exercise 58.

A light-year is the number of miles traveled by light in 1 year (365 days). The speed of light is approximately 186,000 miles per second.

  1. Compute the number of miles in 1 light-year, and express your answer in scientific notation.

  2. The star nearest to the Sun is Proxima Centauri, at a distance of 4.3 light-hears. How long would it take Pioneer 10 (the first space vehicle to achieve escape velocity from the solar system), traveling at 32,114 miles per hour, to reach Proxima Centauri?
