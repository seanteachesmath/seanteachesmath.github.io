Exercise Group 3.2.6.45–50.

For Problems 45–50, use the distributive law to write each product as a sum of power functions.

45.

\(x^{-1}(x^2 - 3x + 2)\)

Answer
46.

\(3x^{-2}(2x^4 +x^2 -4)\)

47.

\(-3t^{-2}(t^2 - 2 + 4t^{-2})\)

Answer
48.

\(-t^{-3}(3t^2 - 1 - t^{-2})\)

49.

\(2u^{-3}(-2u^3 - u^2 + 3u)\)

Answer
50.

\(2u^{-1}(-1 - u - 2u^2)\)
