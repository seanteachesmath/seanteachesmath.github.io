  2. The domain of the natural log function is the same as the range of \(y = e^x\text{,}\) or all positive numbers. The range of \(y = \ln (x)\) is the same as the domain of \(y = e^x\text{,}\) or all real numbers. These results are confirmed by the graph of \(y = \ln(x)\text{.}\)
