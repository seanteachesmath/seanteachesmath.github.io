Example A.10.8.

  1. \(\begin{aligned}[t] \sqrt{3} \left(\sqrt{2x}+\sqrt{6} \right) \amp = \sqrt{3\cdot 2x}+ \sqrt{3\cdot 6}\\ \amp = \sqrt{6x}+\sqrt{18}=\sqrt{6x}+3\sqrt{2} \end{aligned}\)

  2. \(\begin{aligned}[t] \left(\sqrt{x}-\sqrt{y} \right)\left(\sqrt{x}+\sqrt{y} \right) \amp = \sqrt{x^2}+\sqrt{xy}-\sqrt{xy}-\sqrt{y^2}\\ \amp = x-y \end{aligned}\)
