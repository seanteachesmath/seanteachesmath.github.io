Exercise 26.

Pregnant women should monitor their intake of caffeine, because it leaves the body more slowly during pregnancy and can be absorbed by the unborn child through the bloodstream. Caffeine leaves a pregnant woman's body at a rate of \(6.7\%\) each hour.

  1. How long will it take before the \(100\) mg of caffeine in a cup of coffee is reduced to \(50\) mg?

  2. How long will it take before the \(100\) mg of caffeine in a cup of coffee is reduced to \(25\) mg?

  3. Make a rough sketch of the decay function, and compare with the graph in Problem 25.
