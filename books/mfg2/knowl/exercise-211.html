Exercise 50.

Graph \(y = 2.5x - 0.025x^2 - 0.005x^3\text{.}\)

  1. Use your graph to solve \(2.5x - 0.025x^2 - 0.005x^3 = 0\text{.}\)

  2. Press Y= and enter \(Y_2 = -5\text{.}\) Press GRAPH, and you should see the horizontal line \(y = -5\) superimposed on your previous graph. How many solutions does the equation

    \begin{equation*} 2.5x - 0.025x^2 - 0.005x^3 = -5 \end{equation*}

    have? Estimate each solution to the nearest whole number.
