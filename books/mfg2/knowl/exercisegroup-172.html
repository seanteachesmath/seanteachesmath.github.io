Exercise Group 3.5.2.21–24.

For Problems 21–24, write each radical as a power with a fractional exponent.

21.

  1. \(2\sqrt[3]{x^2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{1}{4}\sqrt[4]{x} \)

Answer
22.

  1. \(z^2\sqrt{z} \)

  2. \(z\sqrt[3]{z} \)

23.

  1. \(\dfrac{6}{\sqrt[4]{b^3}} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{-1}{3\sqrt[3]{b}} \)

Answer
24.

  1. \(\dfrac{-4}{(\sqrt[4]{a})^2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{2}{(\sqrt{a})^3} \)
