For the third case, we choose \(a = 3\) and \(b = -5\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \abs{3 + (-5)}=\abs{-2} = 2 ~\text{ and } ~ \abs{3}+\abs{-5}=3 + 5 = 8 \end{equation*}

so \(\abs{3 + (-5)} \lt \abs{3}+\abs{-5}\text{.}\) In each case, \(\abs{a + b}\le\abs{a} +\abs{b}\text{.}\)
