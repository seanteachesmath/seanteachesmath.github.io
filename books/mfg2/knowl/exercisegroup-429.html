Exercise Group A.6.7.1–8.

For Problems 1–8, simplify by applying the appropriate law of exponents.

1.

  1. \(b^4\cdot b^5\)

  2. \(b^2\cdot b^8\)

  3. \((q^3)(q)(q^5) \)

  4. \((p^2)(p^4)(p^4) \)

2.

  1. \(\dfrac{w^6}{w^3} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{c^{12}}{c^4} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{z^6}{z^9} \)

  4. \(\dfrac{b^4}{b^8} \)

3.

  1. \(2^7\cdot 2^2 \)

  2. \(6^5\cdot 6^3 \)

  3. \(\dfrac{2^9}{2^4} \)

  4. \(\dfrac{8^6}{8^2} \)

4.

  2. \((d^4)^2 \)

  3. \((5^4)^3 \)
