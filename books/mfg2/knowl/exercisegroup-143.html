Exercise Group 3.2.6.41–44.

For Problems 41–44, write each expression as a sum of terms of the form \(kx^p\text{.}\)

41.

  1. \(\dfrac{x}{3}+\dfrac{3}{x} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{x-6x^2}{4x^3} \)

Answer
42.

  1. \(\dfrac{2}{x^2}-\dfrac{x^2}{2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{5x+1}{(3x)^2} \)

43.

  1. \(\dfrac{2}{x^4}\left(\dfrac{x^2}{4}+\dfrac{x}{2}-\dfrac{1}{4} \right) \)

  2. \(\dfrac{x^2}{3}\left(\dfrac{2}{x^4}-\dfrac{1}{3x^2}+\dfrac{1}{2} \right) \)

Answer
44.

  1. \(\dfrac{9}{x^3}\left(\dfrac{x^3}{3}-1-\dfrac{1}{x^3} \right) \)

  2. \(\dfrac{x^2}{2}\left(\dfrac{3}{x}-\dfrac{5}{x^3}+\dfrac{7}{x^5} \right) \)
