Exercise Group 3.2.6.35–40.

For Problems 35–40, use the laws of exponents to simplify and write without negative exponents.

35.

  1. \(a^{-3}\cdot a^8 \)

  2. \(5^{-4} \cdot 5^{-3} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{p^{-7}}{p^{-4}} \)

  4. \(\left(7^{-2} \right)^{5} \)

Answer
36.

  1. \(b^{2}\cdot b^{-6} \)

  2. \(4^{-2} \cdot 4^{-6} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{w^{-9}}{w^{2}} \)

  4. \(\left(9^{-4} \right)^{3} \)

37.

  1. \(\left(4x^{-5} \right)\left(5x^2 \right) \)

  2. \(\dfrac{3u^{-3}}{9u^{9}} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{5^6 t^0}{5^{-2}t^{-1}} \)

Answer
38.

  1. \(\left(3y^{-8} \right)\left(2y^4 \right) \)

  2. \(\dfrac{4c^{-4}}{8c^{-8}} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{3^{10}s^{-1}}{3^{-5}s^0} \)

39.

  1. \(\left(3x^{-2}y^3 \right)^{-2} \)

  2. \(\left(\dfrac{6a^{-3}}{b^{2}} \right)^{-2} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{5h^{-3}(h^4)^{-2}}{6h^{-5}} \)

Answer
40.

  1. \(\left(2x^{3}y^{-4} \right)^{-3} \)

  2. \(\left(\dfrac{a^{4}}{4b^{-5}}\right)^{-3} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{4v^{-5}(v^{-2})^{-4}}{3v^{-8}} \)
