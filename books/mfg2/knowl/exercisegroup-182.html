Exercise Group 4.1.8.23–26.

In Problems 23–26, use the laws of exponents to simplify.

23.

  1. \(3^x \, 3^4\)

  2. \((3^x)^4\)

  3. \(3^x \, 4^x\)

Answer
24.

  1. \(8^x \, 8^x\)

  2. \(8^{x+2} \, 8^{x-1} \)

  3. \(\dfrac{8^{2x}}{8^x} \)

25.

  1. \(b^{-4t} \, b^{2t} \)

  2. \((b^t)^{1/2} \)

  3. \(b^{t-1} \, b^{1-t} \)

Answer
26.

  1. \(b^{t/2} \, b^{t/2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{b^{2t}}{b} \)

  3. \(b^{1/t} \, b^t \)
