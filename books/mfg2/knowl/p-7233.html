The population of the state of New York increased during the years 1990 to 2000 according to the formula

\begin{equation*} P(t) = 17.9905 (10)^{0.0023t}\text{,} \end{equation*}

where \(t\) is measured in years since \(1990\text{.}\)

  1. What was the population in \(2000\text{?}\) Give units in your answer.

  2. Assuming the same rate of growth, estimate the population of New York in millions in the year \(2015\text{.}\)

  3. When did the population of New York reach \(20,000,000\text{?}\)

  4. When should the population reach \(30,000,000\text{?}\)

  5. Graph the function \(P\) with a suitable domain and range, then verify your answers to parts (a) through (d).
