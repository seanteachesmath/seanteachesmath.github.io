Exercise Group 7.6.2.5–8.

For Problems 5–8, find the indicated term.

5.

\((1 - 3x + 5x^2)(7 + x - x^2)\text{;} ~~x^2\)

Answer
6.

\((-3 + x - 4x^2)(4 + 3x - 2x^3)\text{;} ~~x^3\)

7.

\((4x - x^2 + 3x^3)(1 + 4x - 3x^2)\text{;} ~~x^3\)

Answer
8.

\((3 - 2x + 2x^3)(5 + 3x - 2x^2 + 4x^4)\text{;} ~~x^4\)
