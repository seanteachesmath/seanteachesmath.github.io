    \begin{equation*} \frac{1}{x}-\frac{1}{y}\ne\frac{1}{x-y} \end{equation*}

    For example, you can check that for \(x=2\) and \(y=3\text{,}\)

    \begin{equation*} \frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{3}\ne\frac{1}{2-3}=-1 \end{equation*}
