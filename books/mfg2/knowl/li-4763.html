We evaluate the formula for \(FV\) when \(P = 200\text{,}\) \(r = 0.05\text{,}\) \(n = 12\text{,}\) and \(t = 15\text{.}\) Substituting these values into the formula, we find
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] FV \amp=\frac{200\left[\left(1+\frac{0.05}{12}\right)^{12(15)}-1\right]}{\frac{0.05}{12}} \\ \amp = \frac{200[(1.00416)^{180}-1]}{0.0041\overline{6}} \\ \amp = 53,457.79 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
In \(15\) years, Greta's retirement fund will be worth $\(53,457.79\text{.}\)
