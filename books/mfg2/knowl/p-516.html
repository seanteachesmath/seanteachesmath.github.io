In ExampleÂ 1.2.22 you were asked to evaluate the function \(f(x)=4x^2-x+5\) at \(x=2h\text{.}\) We can ask this question using composition notation. Let \(f(x)=4x^2-x+5\) and \(g(h)=2h\text{,}\) and find \((f\circ g)(h)\text{,}\) or, \(f(g(h))\text{.}\) The idea with composition is that you do not only evaluate a function at a point, but at another function. The end result is a new function.
