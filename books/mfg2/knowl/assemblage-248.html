Laws of Exponents.

  1. \(a^m\cdot a^n = a^{m+n}\)

    • \(\dfrac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m-n} \hphantom{blank1}(n\lt m)\)

    • \(\displaystyle{\frac{a^m}{a^n}=\frac{1}{a^{n-m}} \hphantom{blank}(n\gt m)}\)

  3. \(\left(a^m\right)^n=a^{m+n}\)

  4. \((ab)^n=a^n b^n\)

  5. \(\displaystyle{\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n} }\)
