Equilibria satisfy \(N_{t+1}=N_t,\) and we denote them by \(N^*=N_{t+1}=N_t\text{.}\) Given a model \(N_{t+1}=f(N_t)N_t\text{,}\) we can see that equilibria occur at points where \(f(N_t)=1\text{.}\) At values of \(N_t\) where our per capita production takes a value of one, we have equilibria.
