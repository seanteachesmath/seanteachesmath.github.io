Our model for the growth of dandelions over the summer is,

\begin{equation*} N_{t+1}=r N_t, \end{equation*}

where \(N_t\) is the state variable and \(r\) is a parameter that characterizes our population. At the beginning of summer we are lucky enough to have only \(N_0=2\) dandelions in the front yard.
