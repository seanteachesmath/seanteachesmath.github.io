Recall, \(F(G)=rGe^{-kG}\text{,}\) and

\begin{equation*} F'(G)= re^{-kG}(1-kG). \end{equation*}

Evaluated at the equilibrium point, \(G^*=\frac{\ln(r)}{k}\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} F'(G^*)&=re^{-kG^*}(1-kG^*)\\ &=re^{-k\left(\frac{\ln(r)}{k}\right)}\left(1-k\left(\frac{\ln(r)}{k}\right)\right)\\ &=re^{-\ln(r)}\left(1-\ln(r)\right)\\ F'(G^*)&=1-\ln(r)\end{aligned} \end{equation*}
