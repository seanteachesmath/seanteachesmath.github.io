How do structured and unstructured populations differ? From a one dimensional model ignoring plant developmental stage,

\begin{equation*} N_t=r N_t, \end{equation*}

where \(r=s\sigma+a\text{,}\) we wrote a two-dimensional model,

\begin{equation*} P_{t+1}=MP_t, \end{equation*}

where \(P_0=(V_0, R_0)^T\) and

\begin{equation*} M=\left( \begin{array}{cc}0 & s\\\sigma & a\end{array}\right). \end{equation*}
