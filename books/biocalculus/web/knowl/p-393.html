A function and its leading behavior approach \(\infty\) at the same rate, or \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\rightarrow \infty}\frac{f(x)}{f_\infty(x)} = 1.\) Also the leading behavior of a function defined by a ratio, such as \(\displaystyle F(x) = \frac{x^{2}}{4+x^{2}}\) is defined as the ratio of the leading behaviors \(\displaystyle F_{0}(x) = \frac{(x^{2})_{0}}{(4+x^{2})_{0}}\) or \(\displaystyle F_\infty(x) = \frac{(x^{2})_\infty}{(4+x^{2})_\infty}\)
