The slope of the secant line that connects the points \((a, f(a))\) and \((b, f(b))\) on the graph of \(f(t)\) is,
\begin{equation*} m_{sec} = \dfrac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a} = \dfrac{\Delta f}{\Delta t}, \end{equation*}
which we call the average rate of change of \(f(t)\) between \(t~=~a\) and \(t~=~b\text{,}\) in meters per second.
