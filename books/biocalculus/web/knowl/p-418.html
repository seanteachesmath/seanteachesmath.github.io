Compute each of the following limits.
  1. Compute the limit of \(f(x) = {2+x^{10}}\) as \(x\to\infty\text{,}\) or \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}{2+x^{10}} =\underline{\hspace{1.25in}}\text{.}\)
  2. Compute the limit of \(f(x) = {x^{-4}}\) as \(x\to\infty\text{,}\) or \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}{x^{-4}} =\underline{\hspace{1.25in}}\text{.}\)
  3. Compute the limit of \(f(x) = {1.7^{x}}\) as \(x\to\infty\text{,}\) or \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}{1.7^{x}} =\underline{\hspace{1.25in}}\text{.}\) Hint: Write \(f(x)\) as an exponential function with base \(e\) and apply our rules for the exponential function.
  4. Compute the limit of \(\displaystyle f(x) = {1+e^{3x}}\) as \(x\to\infty\text{,}\) or \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}{1+e^{3x}} =\underline{\hspace{1.25in}}\text{.}\)
  5. Compute the limit of \(\displaystyle f(x) = {x^{-0.6}}\) as \(x\to\infty\text{,}\) or \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\to\infty}{x^{-0.6}} =\underline{\hspace{1.25in}}\text{.}\)
