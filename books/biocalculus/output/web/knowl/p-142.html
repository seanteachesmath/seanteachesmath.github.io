\begin{equation*} m=\frac{y_{1}-y_{0}}{x_{1}-x_{0}} \end{equation*}

gives us all we need to derive both point-slope form and slope-intercept form. Let our point be \((x_{0}, y_{0})\) and instead of \((x_{1}, y_{1})\text{,}\) choose \((x, y)\) to be any other point on the line.
