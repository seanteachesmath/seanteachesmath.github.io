Provided \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a} g(x) \ne 0\text{,}\) the limit of the quotient (or ratio) is the quotient (or ratio) of the limits

\begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to a}\left( \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right)=\frac{\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a} f(x)}{\displaystyle\lim_{x\to a} g(x)}. \end{equation*}
