To introduce the inverse function it is helpful to think of powers of ten. Consider

\begin{equation*} 1,000,000 = 10^6 \end{equation*}

To write one million, we multiply \(6\) factors of \(10\text{.}\) We say, “\(10\) raised to the power \(6\) gives \(1,000,000\text{,}\)” or, symbolically
