An oil company describes the rate of change in consumption of oil resources (in billions of barrels) using the equation

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{dc}{dt} = 1.2e^{0.04t} \end{equation*}

with \(t = 0\) indicating the year 1998.

  1. Using a definite integral, compute the total resource consumption over the first ten years since 1998. Note: If you use \(u\)-substitution, be sure to transform the limits of integration, otherwise change back to the original variable!

  2. What is the average rate of consumption over the period?

  3. Repeat these two steps for the next ten years, the period from 2008 to 2018.
