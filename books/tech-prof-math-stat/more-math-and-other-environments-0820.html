Section 1.2 More math and other environments

Subsection 1.2.1 Preview

We will continue typing mathematical content and learning about some other ‘environments’ in LaTeX to achieve particular formats for our written content.

Subsection 1.2.2 Review

Today we opened up our template from Day 1 and continued with the ‘list’ environments itemize INDEX LaTeX , list , ‘itemize’ and enumerate . We reviewed the different subtleties of math display, then to move forward from single terms or equations ended up on the aligned math environments align and align*. It was important to note that & was required as our alignment anchor, otherwise things did not actually align very well.

Miscellaneous tasks included making a table of contents and adjusting text spacing with , , and more aggressively newpage .
