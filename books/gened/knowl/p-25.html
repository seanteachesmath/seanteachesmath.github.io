Rational numbers are all numbers that can be written as fractions of whole numbers (integers). These may `terminate' as in \(\sfrac{1}{4} = 0.25\) or repeat forever as single digits \(\sfrac{1}{3} = 0.333\dots=0.\overline{3}\) or patterns \(\sfrac{3}{11} = 0.2727\dots=0.\overline{27}\text{.}\)
