Item 1.

The coin is balanced and \(P(\text{success}) = P(\text{failure}) = 0.5\text{.}\) We are looking to evaluate the binomial probability, sometimes written \(\operatorname{Bin}(n=9, \theta=0.5)\text{,}\) specifically \(b(5; 9, 0.5)\text{.}\) This is,

\begin{equation*} b(5; 9, 0.5) = {9 \choose 5}0.5^5(1-0.5)^{9-5} \approx 0.2461 \end{equation*}
