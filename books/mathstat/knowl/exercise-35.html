Exercise 2. Problem 4.10.

\begin{equation*} f(x) = \begin{cases} \dfrac{1}{x\ln(3)}, \amp \quad 1 \lt x \lt 3\\ 0, \amp \quad \text{elsewhere}\end{cases} \end{equation*}

  1. find \(E[X]\text{,}\) \(E[X^2]\text{,}\) \(E[X^3]\text{.}\)

  2. determine the value of \(E[X^3 + 2X^2-3X+1]\text{.}\)
